The Case File

⚠ THE SOLUTION: Tuner Tan (sound engineer) · poisoned coffee · in the Control Room. He moved the body to the Live Studio afterwards and set the transmitter to static so the death would look like an on-air heart attack.

What actually happened (reveal-skit script basis)

Sound engineer Tuner Tan built this station's sound for 15 years. Mad Max humiliated him on air, stole credit for his work, and got him passed over for Chief Engineer. On the night of the annual Golden Mic broadcast, Tan laced the thermos of coffee he always prepares for Max. Max stormed into the Control Room during the midnight break to yell at Tan again — and drank it there. Tan dragged the body back to the Live Studio chair, put the headphones on him, and switched the transmitter to static.

But he left traces: drag marks he couldn't fully wipe, a coffee ring on the mixing desk, and the broadcast log proving the studio mic had been dead since 10 pm — so Max was never "on air" when he died.
