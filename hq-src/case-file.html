What really happened (base for the reveal-skit script)真相还原（真相揭晓短剧的剧本依据）

Sound engineer Tuner Tan has run this station's sound for 15 years. Mad Max shamed him on air, took credit for his work, and blocked him from becoming Chief Engineer. On the night of the yearly Golden Mic broadcast, Tan put poison in the thermos of coffee he always prepares for Max. During the midnight break, Max stormed into the Control Room to yell at Tan again — and drank the coffee there. Tan dragged the body back to the Live Studio chair, put the headphones on him, and switched the transmitter to static.音响工程师 Tuner Tan 在这家电台做了 15 年的声音。Mad Max 在直播里羞辱他、抢他的功劳，还害他升不上总工程师。一年一度 Golden Mic 直播那晚，Tan 在他每晚照常帮 Max 准备的那壶咖啡里下了毒。午夜休息时间，Max 冲进 Control Room 又要骂 Tan——就在那里把咖啡喝了下去。Tan 把尸体拖回 Live Studio 的椅子上，帮他戴上耳机，再把发射机调成杂音。

But he left traces: drag marks he could not fully wipe away, a coffee ring on the mixing desk, and the broadcast log showing the studio mic had been dead since 10 pm — so Max was never "on air" when he died.但他还是留下了痕迹：擦不干净的拖痕、混音台上的一圈咖啡渍，还有广播日志——上面写明直播室的麦克风晚上 10 点就坏了——所以 Max 死的时候，根本没有在"直播"。
