What actually happened (reveal-skit script basis)真相还原（真相揭晓短剧的剧本依据）

Sound engineer Tuner Tan built this station's sound for 15 years. Mad Max humiliated him on air, stole credit for his work, and got him passed over for Chief Engineer. On the night of the annual Golden Mic broadcast, Tan laced the thermos of coffee he always prepares for Max. Max stormed into the Control Room during the midnight break to yell at Tan again — and drank it there. Tan dragged the body back to the Live Studio chair, put the headphones on him, and switched the transmitter to static.音响工程师 Tuner Tan 为这家电台打磨了 15 年的声音。Mad Max 在直播中羞辱他、抢走他的功劳，还害他与总工程师的位子失之交臂。年度 Golden Mic 直播当晚，Tan 在自己每晚照例为 Max 准备的保温壶咖啡里下了毒。午夜休息时段，Max 冲进 Control Room 又要骂 Tan——就在那里把咖啡喝了下去。Tan 把尸体拖回 Live Studio 的椅子上，给他戴上耳机，再把发射机切成杂音。

But he left traces: drag marks he couldn't fully wipe, a coffee ring on the mixing desk, and the broadcast log proving the studio mic had been dead since 10 pm — so Max was never "on air" when he died.但他留下了痕迹：擦不干净的拖拽痕、混音台上的一圈咖啡渍，还有广播日志——证明直播室麦克风从晚上 10 点起就坏了——所以 Max 死的时候根本不在"直播中"。
