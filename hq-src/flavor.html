Flavor Pack — the 20% that makes it unforgettable气氛包——让人念念不忘的那 20%

Nothing here is required to run the game. Everything here is what people will still be talking about next year. Steal what fits your crew's energy.这里的东西没有一样是游戏运行的必需品，但明年大家还在津津乐道的，全是这里的东西。看你们筹委的气场，合适的尽管拿去用。

Opening skit — full staging (5 min of the 15)开场短剧——完整舞台调度（15 分钟里的 5 分钟）

1
Blackout + static.全场熄灯＋电流杂音。

Hall lights off. 10 seconds of loud static on the PA (any white-noise clip). Let it get uncomfortable. Then cut to dead silence — the silence is the jump-scare.大厅灯全关。PA 放 10 秒响亮的电流杂音（随便一段白噪音都行）。让它响到大家开始不安，然后突然切断——那一瞬间的死寂才是真正的惊吓点。

2
The discovery.发现现场。

One flashlight beam finds the Intern (in costume) frozen mid-scream next to the taped body outline, holding a dropped coffee tray. Hold the tableau 3 seconds. Emcee's cold open starts over this image.一束手电筒光打在 The Intern（穿好戏服）身上：他端着摔落的咖啡托盘，僵在胶带人形旁边，尖叫定格到一半。画面保持 3 秒。司仪的冷开场就压着这幅画面开始。

3
The suspect parade.嫌疑人巡游。

As the emcee names each suspect, that room master steps into a flashlight beam one at a time, does one signature gesture (Stella lowers her sunglasses; Reed checks his watch; Tan wipes his hands on his cloth; Echo scribbles; Joe strums one sour note; the janitor points at their throat), then walks off toward their room. Now every team knows every face.司仪每念到一个嫌疑人，对应的关主就依次走进手电筒光束，做一个标志性动作（Stella 压低墨镜；Reed 看表；Tan 用抹布擦手；Echo 奋笔疾书；Joe 拨错一个走音的音；清洁工指着自己的喉咙），然后朝自己的房间走去。这样每支侦探小队都认得每张脸了。

4
Lights up, rules, dispatch.开灯、讲规则、派遣出发。

Notebooks handed out DURING the rules brief so idle hands read the grid while listening. Teams dispatched by PA in radio-speak: "Unit One to the Live Studio. Unit Two to the Control Room…"讲规则的『同时』就把笔记本发下去，让闲不住的手边听边研究排除表。用电台腔通过 PA 派遣队伍："Unit One，前往 Live Studio。Unit Two，前往 Control Room……"

Fake sponsor ads — emcee filler between announcements假冠名广告——司仪在广播之间的垫场

Read one every ~20 minutes in a golden-age announcer voice. They keep the PA warm, the world alive, and two of them are secretly clues.每隔约 20 分钟，用黄金年代播音腔读一条。它们让 PA 一直有声音、让世界观活着——而且其中两条偷偷藏着线索。

Nudge toward the Green Room for teams that haven't found the split clue.给还没找到拆分线索的队伍一点暗示，把他们往 Green Room 推一推。

Use as the 1:45 time call. Cruel. Effective.用作 1:45 的时间提醒。残忍。有效。

Fun awards for the reveal (beyond top 3)真相揭晓时的趣味奖项（前三名之外）

  • The Loudest Wrong — most confident failed accusation (they earned that PA announcement).最响亮的错误奖——最自信的失败正式指控（那条全场 PA 广播是他们应得的）。
  • The Column A Award — best interrogation question a room master reports hearing.A 栏大奖——关主亲耳听到并上报的最佳审问金句。
  • Best Dressed Detective — self-explanatory; campers WILL dress up if you announce this beforehand.最佳侦探着装奖——不解释；只要提前宣布，营员『一定』会盛装出席。
  • The Static Award — the team that chased red herrings most magnificently. Presented with love.杂音奖——把烟雾弹（假线索）追得最壮烈的队伍。饱含爱意地颁发。
  • Golden Snore — anything hilarious that happened at the Green Room. There will be something.金鼾奖——颁给 Green Room 发生的任何爆笑事件。一定会有的。

Photo moments (assign to floater)拍照时刻（交给机动组）

  • Opening tableau (Intern + coffee tray) — shoot it at rehearsal too, in case the live one is dark.开场定格画面（The Intern＋咖啡托盘）——彩排时也拍一次，以防正式那场太暗。
  • Every team's first clue card — mugshot style against a wall, holding the card like an evidence board.每支队伍的第一张线索卡——靠墙拍"嫌犯大头照"风格，把卡片举得像证物牌一样。
  • The moment after each WRONG announcement. Faces. Priceless.每次广播"指控错误"之后的那一刻。那些表情。无价。
  • Winning team recreates the crime scene with the actual solution for the closing slide.获胜队伍按真正的答案重演犯罪现场，拍下来做闭幕投影。
Energy rule for all crew: commit to the bit. A committee that plays it straight-faced and slightly too seriously is 10× funnier than one that winks. The campers break character FOR you — never the other way around.全体工作人员的能量法则：把戏演到底。一支面无表情、认真得有点过头的筹委，比挤眉弄眼的好笑十倍。要让营员先为你笑场——永远不能反过来。
