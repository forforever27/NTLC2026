Flavor Pack — the 20% people will never forget气氛包——让大家忘不了的那 20%

You don't need anything on this page to run the game. But this is the stuff people will still talk about next year. Take whatever fits your crew's style.这里的东西，没有一样是游戏跑起来一定要有的。但明年大家还会一直讲的，全是这里的东西。看你们筹委的风格，觉得合适的尽管拿去用。

Opening skit — how to set it up, step by step (5 min of the 15)开场短剧——完整做法（15 分钟里占 5 分钟）

1
Blackout + static.全场熄灯＋电流杂音。

Turn off all the hall lights. Play 10 seconds of loud static on the PA (any white-noise clip works). Let it get uncomfortable. Then cut it dead — the sudden silence is the real scare.大厅的灯全关。PA 放 10 秒很大声的杂音（随便一段白噪音都行）。让它响到大家开始不舒服，然后突然切掉——那一下的安静才是真正吓人的地方。

2
The discovery.发现现场。

One flashlight beam finds the Intern (in costume) next to the taped body outline, holding a spilled coffee tray, frozen mid-scream like a photo. Hold that freeze for 3 seconds. The emcee starts the opening story over this image.一束手电筒的光照到 The Intern（穿好戏服）身上：他端着打翻的咖啡托盘，僵在胶带人形旁边，尖叫到一半定格。这个画面停 3 秒。司仪就对着这个画面开始讲开场白。

3
The suspect parade.嫌疑人一个个亮相。

Each time the emcee names a suspect, that room master steps into the flashlight beam, does one special move (Stella lowers her sunglasses; Reed checks his watch; Tan wipes his hands on his cloth; Echo writes like crazy; Joe plays one sour note; the janitor points at their throat), then walks off toward their room. Now every team knows every face.司仪每念到一个嫌疑人，那个关主就走进手电筒的光里，做一个招牌动作（Stella 压低墨镜；Reed 看手表；Tan 用抹布擦手；Echo 埋头狂写；Joe 拨错一个走音的音；清洁工指着自己的喉咙），然后走向自己的房间。这样每一队都认得每张脸了。

4
Lights up, rules, dispatch.开灯、讲规则、出发。

Hand out the notebooks WHILE you explain the rules, so restless hands read the grid while listening. Send teams out over the PA in radio style: "Unit One to the Live Studio. Unit Two to the Control Room…"讲规则的『同时』就把笔记本发下去，让手闲不住的人边听边看排除表。用电台的口吻通过 PA 叫队伍出发："Unit One，去 Live Studio。Unit Two，去 Control Room……"

Fake sponsor ads — for the emcee to fill gaps between announcements假广告——司仪在广播之间用来填空的

Read one every ~20 minutes in an old-time radio announcer voice. They keep the PA busy and the story feeling real — and two of them are secretly clues.每隔大概 20 分钟，用老式播音员的腔调念一条。它们让 PA 一直有声音、让整个故事像真的——而且其中两条偷偷藏着线索。

Golden Mic CoffeeGolden Mic Coffee 金话筒咖啡

"This investigation is brought to you by GOLDEN MIC COFFEE. Rich. Dark. Unforgettable. Golden Mic Coffee — the last cup you'll ever need.""本次调查由 GOLDEN MIC COFFEE 金话筒咖啡冠名播出。够浓。够黑。一喝忘不了。Golden Mic Coffee——你这辈子需要的『最后』一杯。"

…this is secretly the murder method. Nobody notices until the reveal. Beautiful.……其实就是凶手用来杀人的方法。真相揭晓之前没人会发现。绝了。

Wraith & Sons MoversWraith & Sons 搬运公司

"Heavy load? Awkward shape? Need it moved QUIETLY, at night, no questions asked? Wraith & Sons. We drag — pardon — we CARRY your burdens away.""东西太重？形状很奇怪？要在半夜『静静』搬走、一句都不多问？找 Wraith & Sons 搬运公司。我们拖——不好意思——我们帮您『扛』走一切重担。"

…this is secretly about the body being moved. Same trick, used twice.……其实讲的就是那具被搬走的尸体。同一招，玩两层。

Dr. Static's LozengesDr. Static's Lozenges 静电博士润喉糖

"Lost your voice at the worst possible moment? Dr. Static's Throat Lozenges! Because some witnesses have so much to say… and no way to say it.""在最要命的时候讲不出话？快用 Dr. Static's 静电博士润喉糖！因为有些证人啊……一肚子话想讲，偏偏一个字都讲不出来。"

A small push toward the Green Room, for teams that haven't found the split clue yet.给还没找到那对『一半一半的线索』的队伍一点暗示，把他们往 Green Room 推一推。

1926 Time Signal1926 报时信号

"At the tone, the time will be… LATER THAN YOU THINK. Beeeep. This has been the Radio 1926 time signal.""听到提示音后，现在的时间是……比你想的『更晚』。哔——。以上是 Radio 1926 报时。"

Use it as the 1:45 time reminder. Mean. It works.拿来做 1:45 的时间提醒。很坏。很有用。

The station jingle电台台歌

♪ "Nine-teen-twen-ty-six… the SIGNAL in the static, the TRUTH between the ticks… Raaaadio 1926!" ♪♪ "一九二六，一九二六……杂音里有信号，滴答间藏真相……Raaaadio 1926！" ♪

Teach the whole crowd during the opening. Sing it before every PA announcement. By hour two, the campers will sing the reply line by themselves — congratulations, you made a song stuck in everyone's head. (Jingle Joe should keep singing it badly in his room, non-stop, in the wrong key.)开场时就教全场唱。每次 PA 广播前都唱一遍。到第二个小时，营员会自己接下一句——恭喜，你们做出了一首洗脑神曲。（Jingle Joe 要在自己房间里不停地、用走音的调子把它唱到烂。）

Fun awards for the reveal (beyond top 3)揭晓真相时的搞笑奖项（前三名以外）

  • The Loudest Wrong — the team that accused with the most confidence and got it the most wrong (they earned that PA announcement).最响亮的错误奖——指认凶手指认得最有信心、结果错得最大声的那队（那条全场广播是他们应得的）。
  • The Column A Award — the best question a room master heard a team ask a suspect.A 栏大奖——关主亲耳听到、报上来的最佳盘问金句。
  • Best Dressed Detective — no explanation needed; if you announce this early, campers WILL dress up.最佳侦探造型奖——不用解释；只要提前宣布，营员『一定』会打扮到全套来。
  • The Static Award — the team that chased the fake clues the hardest. Given with love.杂音奖——追假线索追得最轰轰烈烈的队伍。带着满满的爱颁发。
  • Golden Snore — for anything funny that happened at the Green Room. Trust us, there will be something.金鼾奖——颁给 Green Room 发生的任何爆笑事件。放心，一定会有的。
The jail choir: every wrong accusation produces a recorded hymn-with-actions from the holding cell. Collect them all and play the best one (or a montage) at the reveal — it may end up being the highlight of the night.牢房诗班：每一次指认错误，都会在拘留区生出一段带动作的诗歌录像。全部收集起来，真相揭晓时播最精彩的一段（或剪成合集）——很可能是当晚的最高潮。

Photos to take (floater's job)必拍的画面（交给机动组）

  • The opening freeze-frame (Intern + coffee tray) — take one at rehearsal too, in case the real one is too dark.开场那个定格画面（The Intern＋咖啡托盘）——彩排时也拍一张，怕正式那场太暗。
  • Every team with its first clue card — pose them against a wall like a police mugshot, holding the card like an evidence sign.每队拿到的第一张线索卡——靠墙拍成"嫌犯大头照"的样子，把卡举得像证物牌。
  • The moment right after each "WRONG" announcement. The faces. Priceless.每次广播"猜错了"之后的那一刻。那些表情。无价。
  • The winning team acts out the crime scene again, this time with the real answer — use it as the closing slide.赢的那队照真正的答案重演一次案发现场，拍下来当闭幕投影。
Energy rule for all crew: act it out all the way. A committee that stays straight-faced and a bit too serious is 10× funnier than one that keeps winking at the joke. The campers should be the first to crack up laughing — never you.全体工作人员的一条定律：把戏演到底。一班一脸认真、认真到有点过头的筹委，比一直挤眉弄眼的好笑十倍。要让营员先笑场——永远不能反过来。
