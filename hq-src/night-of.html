If teams cross-check stories

  • All alibis except Tan's hold up under any questioning — encourage cross-checking, it makes clue cards land harder.
  • Tan's lie is exactly one sentence: "Then he left." If a sharp team pushes ("did anyone SEE Max leave the Control Room?") every other character truthfully answers "no". That's a legitimate soft path to the answer — reward it with respect, not extra clues.
  • Never invent new events. If asked something not on this page: "I don't recall. It was a long night." — then redirect to your mini-game.
