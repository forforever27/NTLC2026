Dead Air — What this game isDead Air——这是个什么游戏

In one sentence: 8 teams of campers race through 7 themed rooms, win a mini-game in each room to earn a clue card, and use those clues to be the first to correctly accuse WHO killed the DJ, HOW, and WHERE.一句话说明：8 支侦探小队穿梭于 7 个主题房间，在每个房间赢下小游戏换取一张线索卡，再用这些线索抢先正确指控：是谁杀了 DJ、用什么作案手法、在哪个案发地点。

The story the players see营员看到的故事

It's midnight at Radio 1926 FM. The station's star DJ — Maxwell "Mad Max" Loh, famous for filling the airwaves with gossip, hype and noise — is found dead in the Live Studio, headphones on, the transmitter broadcasting pure static. Six station staff were in the building. Every one of them had a reason. The teams are detectives called in before the police arrive.午夜，Radio 1926 FM。电台王牌 DJ——Maxwell "Mad Max" Loh，以用八卦、炒作和噪音塞满大气电波而闻名——被发现死在 Live Studio 里：头戴耳机，发射机播着一片纯粹的杂音。案发当晚有六名电台员工在楼里，每一个都有动机。各侦探小队就是在警察抵达之前受邀介入的侦探。

How it plays, step by step玩法：一步一步来

1
Opening (15 min).开场（15 分钟）。

Static blasts over the PA. Emcee narrates the discovery; helpers stage the crime scene. Each team receives a Detective Notebook — a Cluedo-style grid of all suspects, methods and locations, which they cross off as clues come in.PA 里炸出一阵刺耳杂音。主持人旁白发现尸体的经过；工作人员布置犯罪现场。每队领到一本侦探笔记本——一张 Cluedo 式的总表，列出所有嫌疑人、作案手法和案发地点，线索到手就逐项划掉。

2
Investigation (2 hours).调查（2 小时）。

Teams roam freely between 7 rooms. Each room = one costumed committee member (a suspect or witness) + one mini-game. One team in a room at a time, ~8–10 minutes per attempt.各队在 7 个房间之间自由走动。每个房间 = 一位穿戏服的筹委（嫌疑人或目击者）+ 一个小游戏。一个房间一次只接待一队，每次挑战约 8–10 分钟。

3
Win the mini-game → get the clue card.赢下小游戏 → 拿线索卡。

Each room pays out exactly one clue: it eliminates suspects, methods, or locations ("Stella was live on Channel 2 all night — innocent"). A team can only clear each room once.每个房间只发放一条线索：它会排除某些嫌疑人、作案手法或案发地点（"Stella 整晚都在 2 号频道直播——清白"）。每队每个房间只能通关一次。

4
Trade to complete the split clue.交换才能凑齐拆分线索。

The Green Room clue comes in two halves held by different teams — they must find each other and trade. This is deliberate: it forces inter-team interaction.Green Room 的线索分成两半，落在不同队伍手里——他们必须找到对方进行交换。这是刻意设计的：逼各队互动。

5
Accuse.指控。

From the 45-minute mark, any team may go to the HQ desk and formally accuse (who + how + where). Wrong: 15-minute accusation lock-out, announced over the PA for drama. Right: they win. Game continues for 2nd/3rd. Reveal skit and debrief close the session.从第 45 分钟起，任何队伍都可以到 HQ 台前正式指控（嫌疑人 + 作案手法 + 案发地点）。猜错：15分钟禁赛，并通过 PA 全场广播，戏剧效果拉满。猜对：获胜。游戏继续决出第二、三名。最后以真相揭晓短剧和主题总结收尾。

Why it can't break为什么这游戏坏不了

No team can fluke it没人能瞎蒙过关

The full answer needs all 7 clues for certainty. Guessing early is possible but risky — the lock-out and public announcement make a wrong guess costly and funny.要百分百确定完整答案，必须集齐全部 7 条线索。提前乱猜不是不行，但风险很高——禁赛加全场广播，让猜错这件事又亏又好笑。

No team can stall没人会卡死

The HQ desk sells a duplicate of any clue card for a 10-minute wait ("developing the film"). Slow teams always have a path forward.HQ 台可以补发任何一张线索卡的副本，代价是等 10 分钟（"照片还在冲洗中"）。落后的队伍永远有路可走。

No traffic jams不会塞车

Teams start at staggered rooms (Team 1 → Room 1, Team 2 → Room 2 …). Room masters cap occupancy at one team and keep attempts under ~10 min.各队从错开的房间出发（第 1 队 → 1 号房，第 2 队 → 2 号房……）。关主控制一个房间一次只进一队，每次挑战不超过约 10 分钟。

Everyone plays人人都要上场

Mini-games require the whole team (relay, recording where all speak, charades). No game is solvable by one smart kid while seven watch.小游戏都需要全队参与（接力、全员开口的录音、比手画脚）。没有哪个游戏能让一个聪明孩子单干、其余七个人围观。

The camp theme tie-in与营会主题的连接

"Unplug from the noise, sync with the Spirit." Mad Max filled the airwaves with noise; every suspect's motive is real, every red herring plausible — that's the noise. Teams win by tuning it out until only the true signal remains. The debrief makes this connection in two minutes, one verse, no sermon. The whole game is the theme."关掉噪音，对准圣灵的频率。"Mad Max 用噪音塞满了大气电波；每个嫌疑人的动机都是真的，每个烟雾弹（假线索）都说得通——这些就是噪音。获胜之道，是把噪音一一滤掉，直到只剩下真正的信号。主题总结只需两分钟、一节经文，不用讲道。整个游戏本身就是主题。
