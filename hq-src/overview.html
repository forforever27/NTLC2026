Dead Air — What this game isDead Air——这是个什么游戏

In one sentence: 8 teams of campers run through 7 themed rooms. Win the mini-game in each room to get a clue card. Use the clues to be the first to guess right: WHO killed the DJ, HOW, and WHERE.一句话讲完：8 队营员跑 7 个主题房间，每个房间赢一个小游戏，就拿一张线索卡。谁先用线索猜对——是谁杀了 DJ、用什么方法杀的、在哪里下手——谁就赢。

The story the players see营员看到的故事

It's midnight at Radio 1926 FM. The station's star DJ — Maxwell "Mad Max" Loh, known for filling the radio with gossip, hype and noise — is found dead in the Live Studio. His headphones are still on. The transmitter is playing pure static. Six station staff were in the building that night. Every one of them had a reason to want him dead. The teams are detectives, called in before the police arrive.半夜，Radio 1926 FM。电台的王牌 DJ——Maxwell "Mad Max" Loh，出了名整天用八卦、炒作和噪音霸占电波——被发现死在 Live Studio 里：耳机还戴着，发射机播的全是杂音。那晚楼里有六个电台员工，每个人都有理由想他死。你们这些小队，就是警察来之前先被请来查案的侦探。

How it plays, step by step玩法：一步一步来

1
Opening (15 min).开场（15 分钟）。

Loud static plays over the PA. The emcee tells everyone how the body was found; helpers set up the crime scene. Each team gets a Detective Notebook — a Cluedo-style grid of all suspects, methods and locations. Teams cross items off as clues come in.PA 突然爆出一阵刺耳杂音。主持人讲出尸体是怎么被发现的；工作人员摆好犯罪现场。每队拿到一本侦探笔记本——像 Cluedo 那样的表格，列出所有嫌疑人、手法和地点。线索一到手，就一项一项划掉。

2
Investigation (2 hours).调查（2 小时）。

Teams move freely between the 7 rooms. Each room = one committee member in costume (a suspect or witness) + one mini-game. One team in a room at a time, about 8–10 minutes per try.各队在 7 个房间随便走。每个房间 = 一个穿戏服的筹委（嫌疑人或目击者）+ 一个小游戏。一个房间一次只进一队，玩一次大概 8–10 分钟。

3
Win the mini-game → get the clue card.赢了小游戏 → 拿线索卡。

Each room gives exactly one clue. It rules out some suspects, methods, or locations ("Stella was live on Channel 2 all night — not her"). A team can only clear each room once.每个房间只给一条线索：帮你划掉一些嫌疑人、手法或地点（"Stella 整晚都在 2 号频道直播——不是她"）。每队每个房间只能过一次。

4
Trade to complete the split clue.要交换，才凑得齐那条一半一半的线索。

The Green Room clue comes in two halves, held by two different teams — they must find each other and trade. This is on purpose: it makes teams talk to each other.Green Room 的线索分成两半，在两个不同的队手上——一定要找到对方，换一换才凑得齐。这是故意的：逼你们跟别队讲话。

5
Accuse.指认凶手。

From the 45-minute mark, any team can go to the HQ desk and accuse (say who + how + where). Wrong: the whole team goes into the HQ holding cell for up to 15 minutes — where they must record a church hymn with actions; a good performance gets them out early — and it's announced over the PA, for drama. Right: they win. The game keeps going for 2nd and 3rd place. A reveal skit and a short wrap-up end the session.从第 45 分钟开始，任何队都可以去 HQ 台指认凶手（是谁 + 用什么方法 + 在哪里下手）。猜错：全队进 HQ 拘留区，最多关 15 分钟——要在里面录一首带动作的诗歌，表现好就提早放人——还会用 PA 广播给全场听，够戏剧。猜对：就赢了。游戏照跑，比出第二、第三名。最后是真相揭晓短剧和主题总结。

Why it can't break为什么这游戏坏不了

No team can win by wild guessing没人能瞎蒙过关

To be 100% sure of the answer, a team needs all 7 clues. Guessing early is allowed but risky — a wrong guess means no guessing for 15 minutes, plus a PA announcement to the whole camp. Painful and funny.要百分百确定答案，7 条线索一条都不能少。早早乱猜也可以，但很亏——15 分钟内不能再猜，还要被全场广播，又丢脸又好笑。

No team can stall没人会卡死

The HQ desk gives out a copy of any clue card — the price is up to 10 minutes on the HQ bench, coloring a team picture while "the film develops" (a great picture gets released early). Teams that fall behind always have a way forward.在 HQ 台可以补拿任何一张线索卡，条件是在 HQ 长凳上最多等 10 分钟——"照片还在冲洗"的时候全队一起涂一张画（涂得好提早放行）。落后的队一定有路走。

No traffic jams不会塞车

Teams start in different rooms (Team 1 → Room 1, Team 2 → Room 2 …). Room masters let in only one team at a time and keep each try under ~10 min.各队从不同的房间出发（第 1 队 → 1 号房，第 2 队 → 2 号房……）。关主看着门：一个房间一次只进一队，一次玩不超过大概 10 分钟。

Everyone plays人人都要上场

Every mini-game needs the whole team (relay, a recording where everyone speaks, charades). No game can be won by one smart kid while seven others watch.小游戏都要全队一起玩（接力、每个人都要开口的录音、比手画脚）。没有一个游戏可以让一个聪明的人自己搞定、其他七个人在旁边看戏。

The camp theme tie-in跟营会主题的关系

"Unplug from the noise, sync with the Spirit." Mad Max filled the radio with noise. Every suspect's motive is real, and every fake clue is believable — that's the noise. Teams win by filtering it out until only the true signal is left. The wrap-up makes this point in two minutes, with one verse, no sermon. The whole game is the theme."关掉噪音，对准圣灵的频率。"Mad Max 用噪音塞满电波；每个嫌疑人的动机都是真的，每条假线索都讲得通——这些就是噪音。要赢，就要把噪音一个一个滤掉，剩下的才是真正的信号。主题总结只要两分钟、一节经文，不用讲道。整个游戏本身就是主题。
