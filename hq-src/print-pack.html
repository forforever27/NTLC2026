TIP FROM THE BUREAU: suspects lie about only one thing — whether they did it. Everything else they say is true. Compare everyone's stories; if nobody can back up someone's story, go talk to them again.侦探局小贴士：嫌疑人只会在一件事上骗你——就是自己有没有做。其他他们讲的都是真话。把每个人的说法对一对；那个没有人可以帮他证明的，值得再去找他一次。
