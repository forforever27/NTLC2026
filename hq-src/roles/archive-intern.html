The Intern — knows everything, fears everyoneThe Intern —— 什么都知道，谁都害怕

Your character你的角色

Overworked, sleep-deprived intern who knows every secret in the station. You act nervous and evasive — which makes teams suspect you — but you're innocent: you were driving Mr. Reed downtown at midnight. You're nervous because you're terrified of Reed finding out you gossip.加班加到严重缺觉的实习生，电台里的每个秘密你都知道。你表现得紧张兮兮、支支吾吾——所以小队会怀疑你——但你是清白的：午夜时分你正开车送 Mr. Reed 进城。你紧张，是因为你怕死了 Mr. Reed 发现你到处八卦。

Costume服装

Lanyard, armful of files, tired eyes (mess up your hair).挂绳工作证、抱一叠文件、一脸倦容（把头发弄乱）。

Your room你的房间

A shelf/table with taped slot markings and ~25 cardboard records scattered "mid-reshelving". The Archive Rule Card posted on the wall. Dust references matter — this room's clue is about undisturbed dust, so keep the room looking untouched and old.一个架子或桌子，用胶带贴出格位标记，约 25 张纸皮唱片散落一地，营造"上架上到一半"的样子。墙上贴着档案室规则卡。灰尘的细节很重要——这个房间的线索跟"没被动过的灰尘"有关，所以整个房间要保持又旧又没人碰过的感觉。

Your mini-game — "Sort the Stacks"你的小游戏 —— "Sort the Stacks"（唱片归位） HANDMADE

1

Brief: "The night shelver quit MID-SHIFT. If these aren't shelved by morning I'm dead. Rules are on the card. Please. Help me."开场词："夜班上架员做到一半跑路了！这些唱片天亮前上不了架，我就死定了。规则在卡上。拜托。救救我。"

2

Rule card: "Shelve by year, oldest left — but any record whose catalogue number ends in 7 goes in reverse order at the end of its year group." Sorted correctly, the first letters of the artists in shelf order spell CHECK THE DUST.规则卡："按年份上架，最旧的放最左——但编目号以 7 结尾的唱片，要倒序排在它所属年份组的末尾。"排对之后，按架上顺序读歌手名的首字母，会拼出 CHECK THE DUST。

3

They must SAY the hidden phrase to you (not just sort). Then hand Clue Card 3 with: "The dust… yes. Look what I noticed." Stuck >10 min → hint: "The first letters. Read the first letters."他们必须把隐藏的句子亲口说给你听（光排对不算）。然后发放线索卡 3，边发边说："灰尘……对。你们看，我注意到了这个。"卡关超过 10 分钟 → 提示："首字母。读读首字母。"

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Your alibi: drove Reed downtown at midnight (matches the Office clue — point teams to "the boss's office" to verify).你的不在场证明：午夜开车送 Mr. Reed 进城（与 Manager's Office 的线索吻合——让小队去"老板的办公室"核实）。
  • Gossip freely about motives (Stella's cancelled show, Joe's royalties, Echo's spiked story) — you're a motive dispenser. Motives are noise; give generously.尽情八卦各种动机（Stella 被砍的节目、Jingle Joe 的版税、Miss Echo 被压下的报道）——你就是行走的动机发放机。动机都是噪音，放心大方地给。
  • You know nothing about HOW or WHERE the murder happened. Don't invent details.关于作案手法和案发地点，你一无所知。别自己编细节。
  • Never mention Tan's grudge unprompted — only if asked directly about Tan: "He got passed over for Chief Engineer. He was… quiet about it. Too quiet? I didn't say that."绝不主动提 Tuner Tan 的怨气——只有被直接问到 Tan 时才说："总工程师的位子没轮到他。他……很平静。平静得有点过头？我什么都没说。"
