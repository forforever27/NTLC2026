Station 3 · Record Archive

The Intern — knows everything, fears everyone

Your character

Overworked, sleep-deprived intern who knows every secret in the station. You act nervous and evasive — which makes teams suspect you — but you're innocent: you were driving Mr. Reed downtown at midnight. You're nervous because you're terrified of Reed finding out you gossip.

Costume

Lanyard, armful of files, tired eyes (mess up your hair).

Your room

A shelf/table with taped slot markings and ~25 cardboard records scattered "mid-reshelving". The Archive Rule Card posted on the wall. Dust references matter — this room's clue is about undisturbed dust, so keep the room looking untouched and old.

Your mini-game — "Sort the Stacks" HANDMADE

1

Brief: "The night shelver quit MID-SHIFT. If these aren't shelved by morning I'm dead. Rules are on the card. Please. Help me."

2

Rule card: "Shelve by year, oldest left — but any record whose catalogue number ends in 7 goes in reverse order at the end of its year group." Sorted correctly, the first letters of the artists in shelf order spell CHECK THE DUST.

3

They must SAY the hidden phrase to you (not just sort). Then hand Clue Card 3 with: "The dust… yes. Look what I noticed." Stuck >10 min → hint: "The first letters. Read the first letters."

What you know / never reveal

  • Your alibi: drove Reed downtown at midnight (matches the Office clue — point teams to "the boss's office" to verify).
  • Gossip freely about motives (Stella's cancelled show, Joe's royalties, Echo's spiked story) — you're a motive dispenser. Motives are noise; give generously.
  • You know nothing about HOW or WHERE the murder happened. Don't invent details.
  • Never mention Tan's grudge unprompted — only if asked directly about Tan: "He got passed over for Chief Engineer. He was… quiet about it. Too quiet? I didn't say that."
