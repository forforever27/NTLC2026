The Intern — knows everything, fears everyoneThe Intern —— 什么都知道，谁都害怕

Your character你的角色

An overworked intern with no sleep who knows every secret in the station. You act nervous and keep dodging questions — so teams will suspect you — but you're innocent: at midnight you were driving Mr. Reed downtown. You're nervous because you're scared Mr. Reed will find out you gossip about everyone.加班加到没得睡的实习生，电台里每个人的秘密你都知道。你整个人紧张兮兮、讲话吞吞吐吐——所以小队会怀疑你——但你是清白的：半夜 12 点你在开车送 Mr. Reed 进城。你紧张，是因为你超怕 Mr. Reed 发现你到处讲八卦。

Costume服装

Lanyard, armful of files, tired eyes (mess up your hair).挂绳工作证、抱一叠文件、一脸没睡够的样子（头发弄乱一点）。

Your room你的房间

A shelf or table with slots marked out in tape, and 12 cardboard records scattered around like someone stopped halfway through shelving them (all the labels are written out on the Station Props page). The Archive Rule Card goes on the wall. Dust matters — this room's clue is about dust that nobody has touched, so make the room look old and untouched.一个架子或桌子，用胶带贴出一格一格的位置，12 张纸皮唱片丢到到处都是，做出"上架上到一半"的样子（标签内容全部写好了，看关卡道具文案页）。墙上贴档案室规则卡。灰尘很重要——这个房间的线索就是跟"没被动过的灰尘"有关，所以整间房要看起来又旧又没人碰过。

Your mini-game — "Sort the Stacks"你的小游戏 —— "Sort the Stacks"（唱片归位） HANDMADE

1

Say: "The night shelver quit MID-SHIFT. If these aren't shelved by morning, I'm dead. Rules are on the card. Please. Help me."开场词："夜班上架员做到一半跑路了！这些唱片天亮前排不好，我就死定了。规则在卡上。拜托。救救我。"

2

The rule card says: "Shelve by year, oldest left — EXCEPT records whose catalogue number ends in 7: those go at the far right, newest first." When sorted correctly, the first letters of the artist names (read in shelf order) spell CHECK THE DUST.规则卡："照年份排，最旧的放最左边——但编号尾数是 7 的唱片例外：放去最右边，而且越新越前面。"排对之后，照架上的顺序读歌手名字的第一个字母，会拼出 CHECK THE DUST。

3

They must SAY the hidden phrase to you out loud (sorting alone is not enough). Then give them Clue Card 3 and say: "The dust… yes. Look what I noticed." If they're stuck for more than 10 minutes, hint: "The first letters. Read the first letters."他们要把藏起来的那句话亲口讲给你听（只是排对不算过）。然后发线索卡 3，边发边说："灰尘……对。你们看，我早就注意到这个。"卡超过 10 分钟 → 提示："第一个字母。读读看第一个字母。"

(if accused) "ME?! I don't even have time to SLEEP, you think I have time to MURDER?"（有人说你是凶手时）"我？！我连睡觉的时间都没有，你觉得我有时间去杀人？？"

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Your alibi — your proof you were somewhere else: you drove Mr. Reed downtown at midnight. It matches the Office clue — send teams to "the boss's office" to check.你的不在场证明：半夜 12 点开车送 Mr. Reed 进城（跟 Manager's Office 的线索对得上——叫小队去"老板的办公室"确认）。
  • Gossip freely about why people hated Max (Stella's cancelled show, Joe's royalties, Echo's blocked story) — you're the station's gossip machine. These motives are all distractions, so share them generously.尽量八卦大家的动机（Stella 被砍的节目、Jingle Joe 的版税、Miss Echo 被压下来的报道）——你就是行走的八卦机。这些动机其实都是干扰，放心大方地讲。
  • You know nothing about HOW or WHERE Max was killed. Don't make up details.凶手用什么方法杀的、在哪里下手，你完全不知道。千万别自己乱编细节。
  • Never bring up Tan's grudge unless they ask. Only if someone asks directly about Tan, say: "They gave the Chief Engineer job to someone else, not him. He was… quiet about it. Too quiet? I didn't say that."千万别自己主动讲 Tuner Tan 的怨气——除非有人直接问起 Tan，你才说："总工程师的位子没轮到他。他……很平静。平静到有点不正常？我什么都没讲哦。"
