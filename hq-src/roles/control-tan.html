Your character你的角色

Proud, precise engineer. 15 years building this station's sound; humiliated on air by Max, credit stolen, passed over for Chief Engineer. You poisoned his coffee in this very room and moved the body. Your play: the helpful expert — so cooperative that nobody suspects you. One beat too defensive when your desk is questioned.自尊心强、一丝不苟的工程师。你花了 15 年打造这座电台的声音，却被 Max 在直播中当众羞辱、功劳被抢，连总工程师的位子也被人顶了。你就在这个房间里给他的咖啡下了毒，然后移动了尸体。你的演法是：热心的专家——配合到没人会怀疑你。唯独有人质疑你的工作台时，你的防备会多那么一拍。

Your mini-game — "Find the Frequency"你的小游戏 —— "Find the Frequency"（寻找频率） WEB APP

2

Team searches the paper props for the 3 numbers, tunes the slider to each. At a correct frequency the static clears and a voice fragment plays.小队在纸质道具里找出那 3 个数字，把滑杆调到对应频率。调对时，杂音会消失，播出一段人声片段。

3

The 3 fragments assemble into the phrase "THE SIGNAL HIDES IN THE STATIC". They must say it to you → hand Clue Card 2.3 段人声拼成一句话："THE SIGNAL HIDES IN THE STATIC"。他们必须亲口对你说出这句话 → 发放线索卡 2。

Fallback (no laptop): 5 sealed envelopes labelled with frequencies; the right 3 contain message strips, wrong ones contain "STATIC" slips (+1 min wait each).备用方案（没有手提电脑时）：准备 5 个写上频率的密封信封；正确的 3 个装着讯息纸条，错误的装着 "STATIC" 纸条（每开错一个罚等 1 分钟）。

  • Never confess, even to direct accusation. Standard line: "Accuse me at the desk, then. See how that goes for you."绝不认罪，哪怕被当面指控。标准回应："那就去总台正式指控我啊。看看你们会有什么下场。"
  • Never volunteer anything about coffee, the thermos, or the drag marks.绝不主动提起咖啡、保温瓶或拖拽痕迹。
  • Don't overact guilt. One too-quick denial per team, maximum.别把心虚演过头。每支小队最多只给一次"回答得太快"的破绽。
