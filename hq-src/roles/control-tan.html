Tuner Tan — sound engineerTuner Tan —— 音响工程师 YOU ARE THE KILLER

Your character你的角色

A proud engineer who does everything exactly right. You spent 15 years building this station's sound. Max embarrassed you in front of everyone on air, stole your credit, and the Chief Engineer job went to someone else. You poisoned his coffee in this very room, then moved the body. Your act: the helpful expert — so helpful that nobody suspects you. Your only crack: you get a little too defensive when someone asks about your desk.自尊心很强、做事很讲究的工程师。这个电台的声音是你花 15 年一手调出来的，结果 Max 在直播里当众羞辱你、抢你的功劳，连总工程师的位子都轮不到你。你就在这间房给他的咖啡下毒，之后还把尸体搬走了。你的演法：热心的专家——帮到没人会怀疑你。只有别人问起你的工作台时，你才会防备心重那么一点点。

Costume服装

Tool belt or screwdriver in pocket, rolled sleeves, cloth in back pocket.工具腰带或口袋插一把螺丝刀，袖子卷起来，后裤袋塞一块抹布。

Your room你的房间

A mixing-desk table (cardboard knobs are fine), the tuner laptop, and the three frequency-hint props placed so they look natural: poster on the wall (92.6), taped-up memo (98.1), coffee-stained logbook (104.7). One small detail: a faint coffee-ring stain on the desk (draw it) — you pretend you "never noticed" it.调音台桌子（旋钮可以用纸皮自己做）、跑调频器的手提电脑，还有三件藏了频率提示的道具，自然地摆好：墙上的海报（92.6）、贴着的便条（98.1）、沾到咖啡渍的工作日志（104.7）。小细节：桌上画一圈淡淡的咖啡杯印——你自己"从来没注意到"它。

Your mini-game — "Find the Frequency"你的小游戏 —— "Find the Frequency"（寻找频率） WEB APP

1

The laptop runs /props/tuner/. Tell the team: "The old receiver picked up three signals that night. Find the frequencies. They're written down somewhere in this room — I never throw anything away."手提电脑打开 /props/tuner/。跟小队讲："那天晚上，这台旧接收器收到三段信号。把那三个频率找出来。它们就写在这间房的某个角落——我这个人，从来不丢东西。"

2

The team searches the paper props for the numbers — the props hide SIX, but only 3 are real; the decoys (90.5 / 95.3 / 99.9) just play static. When a frequency is right, the static clears and a short voice clip plays. Never say which numbers are fake — the radio does the judging.小队在纸质道具里找数字——道具里其实藏了六个，只有 3 个是真的；假的（90.5 / 95.3 / 99.9）调过去只有杂音。调对的话，杂音会消失，播出一小段人声。哪些是假的你绝对不讲——让收音机自己当裁判。

3

The 3 clips join into one phrase: "THE SIGNAL HIDES IN THE STATIC". They must say it to you out loud → give them Clue Card 2.3 段人声拼起来是一句话："THE SIGNAL HIDES IN THE STATIC"。他们要亲口对你讲出这句话 → 发线索卡 2。

Backup plan (no laptop): 5 sealed envelopes with frequencies written on them. The right 3 hold message strips; the wrong ones hold "STATIC" slips (each wrong pick = wait 1 minute).备用方案（没有手提电脑的话）：准备 5 个写了频率的密封信封；对的 3 个装讯息纸条，错的装 "STATIC" 纸条（开错一个就罚等 1 分钟）。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Really help them with the mini-game — being helpful IS your disguise.小游戏要真心帮忙——热心就是你的伪装。
  • If they ask about Stella: confirm the Channel 2 tape is real (it is — and it moves their attention to the log you just happened to "find again").有人问起 Stella 时：确认 Channel 2 的录音带是有的（这是真话，还可以把大家的注意力带去你"刚好找回来"的那份记录）。
  • Never confess, even if they accuse you to your face. Your standard line: "Accuse me at the desk, then. See how that goes for you."绝对不认罪，就算被当面指着鼻子说是你也一样。标准回应："有本事就去总台指认凶手（正式指控）啊，说是我。看你们会有什么下场。"
  • Never bring up the coffee, the thermos, or the drag marks yourself.千万别自己主动讲到咖啡、保温瓶或地上的拖痕。
  • Don't overdo the guilty act. Give each team ONE too-fast denial, no more.心虚不要演过头。每队最多只给一次"答太快"的破绽。
