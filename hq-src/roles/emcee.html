Crew · Voice of the game筹委 · 游戏之声

Emcee — opening, PA drama, reveal, debrief司仪——开场、广播戏剧、真相揭晓、主题总结

You are the radio announcer voice of the whole evening. Old-timey, theatrical, a little too fond of your own transitions. You carry the three set-pieces: the opening, the announcements, the reveal.你是整个晚上的电台播音员之声。老派、戏剧化、有点太爱自己的转场词。三大重头戏由你扛起：开场、广播播报、真相揭晓。

Opening script (0:00–0:15)开场剧本（0:00–0:15）

1

Cold open: loud static over the PA (10 s), cut to silence. Then, in announcer voice:冷开场：广播里播放响亮的静电噪音（10秒），骤然切到寂静。然后，用播音员的嗓音说：

"Good evening, listeners. This is Radio 1926 FM… or it was. At the stroke of midnight, the voice that never stopped talking — finally stopped. DJ Maxwell 'Mad Max' Loh: found in his studio chair. Headphones on. Microphone live. Broadcasting… nothing. Dead. Air."“晚上好，各位听众。这里是 Radio 1926 FM……或者说，曾经是。午夜钟声敲响之际，那个从不停嘴的声音——终于停了。DJ Maxwell 'Mad Max' Loh：被发现坐在他的播音椅上。耳机戴着。麦克风开着。播出的……是一片虚无。Dead. Air.——死寂的电波。”
2

Setup: "Six members of staff were in the building. Every one of them had a reason. The police are forty minutes away. You — are not." Introduce the three questions: WHO. HOW. WHERE. Hold up the notebook, explain crossing off.铺垫：“当晚有六名员工在大楼里。每一个人，都有动机。警察还要四十分钟才到。而你们——就在现场。”介绍三大问题：是谁（WHO）。怎么下手（HOW）。在哪里（WHERE）。举起侦探笔记本，讲解划格排除法。

3

Rules: read the 7 player rules from the Run Sheet verbatim. Dispatch teams to staggered starting rooms. Start the clock. "The station is yours, detectives. Mind the static."规则：逐字读出 Run Sheet 上的7条营员规则。把各侦探小队分派到错开的起始房间。开始计时。“这座电台交给你们了，侦探们。小心那片静电。”

PA announcements during play游戏进行中的广播播报

  • Failed accusation (radio from HQ):指控失败（HQ 无线电通报）： "Attention all units. Team [N] made a formal accusation… and was WRONG. The killer remains at large. Team [N] is benched from accusing for fifteen minutes. Carry on."“全体单位注意。第[N]小队提出了正式指控……结果——是错的。凶手仍然逍遥法外。第[N]小队十五分钟内禁止再次指控。请继续。”
  • Time calls: at 1:00 ("halfway to the police arriving"), 1:45 ("fifteen minutes to the final window"), 2:15 ("FINAL WINDOW — every team to HQ").报时：1:00（“警察的路程已经过半”）、1:45（“距离最终提交窗口还有十五分钟”）、2:15（“最终提交窗口开启——所有小队到 HQ 集合”）。
  • Winner: dramatic sting moment — coordinate with HQ so the announcement lands right after the terminal's sting.胜出者：戏剧性音效时刻——与 HQ 协调，让宣布紧接在终端音效之后落地。

Reveal script (2:35–3:00)真相揭晓剧本（2:35–3:00）

1

Walk the giant grid clue by clue — cross off in the same order teams did: wounds (card 1), the dead mic and Stella's tape (card 2), the dust (card 3), the telegraph receipt (card 4), the sleeping jingle-man (card 5), the locked storage (card 6), the lawyer's slip (card 7). Land on: Tan. Coffee. Control Room.在巨型排除表上逐条讲解线索——按各小队破案的同样顺序划掉：伤痕（1号线索卡）、失灵的麦克风和 Stella 的录音带（2号卡）、灰尘（3号卡）、电报收据（4号卡）、熟睡的广告歌作者（5号卡）、上锁的储藏室（6号卡）、律师的预约单（7号卡）。最后落在：Tan。毒咖啡。Control Room。

2

Tan's confession (Tan performs, you narrate around it): the humiliation, the thermos, Max storming into the Control Room, the drag marks, the static. Keep it 60–90 seconds.Tan 的自白（Tan 负责表演，你在旁边旁白）：那份屈辱、那个保温瓶、Max 冲进 Control Room、拖拽的痕迹、那片静电。控制在60–90秒。

3

Winners: first correct accusation, then final-window accuracy placings (HQ hands you the terminal ranking). Prizes.胜出者：先颁给第一个正式指控正确的小队，再按最终提交窗口的准确度排名（HQ 会把终端排名交给你）。颁奖。

4

Debrief (2 min, land it and stop):主题总结（2分钟，讲到位就收）：

"Every suspect had a motive. Every rumor was plausible. The room was FULL of noise — and the answer was never in the noise. You solved this by tuning it out until one true signal remained. That's this whole camp in one game: unplug from the noise… sync with the Spirit."“每个嫌疑人都有动机。每个流言听起来都合理。整个房间充满了噪音——而答案，从来不在噪音里。你们能破案，是因为你们把噪音一一调开，直到只剩下一个真实的信号。这就是整个营会浓缩成的一个游戏：关掉噪音……对准圣灵的频率。”

One verse (suggestion: 1 Kings 19:11–12, the still small voice). Pray. Done.读一节经文（建议：列王纪上19:11–12，微小的声音）。祷告。结束。

  • Giant elimination grid + marker for the reveal巨型排除表 + 真相揭晓用的马克笔
