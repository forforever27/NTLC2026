Crew · Voice of the game筹委 · 游戏之声

Emcee — opening, radio drama, the reveal, wrap-up司仪——开场、广播戏剧、真相揭晓、主题总结

You are the radio announcer voice of the whole evening. Old-fashioned, dramatic, a little too in love with your own transitions. You carry the three big moments: the opening, the announcements, the reveal.整个晚上，你就是电台播音员的那把声音。老派、够戏剧、还有点太爱自己的转场词。三场重头戏靠你撑：开场、广播播报、真相揭晓。

Opening script (0:00–0:15)开场剧本（0:00–0:15）

1

Cold open: play loud static over the PA (10 s), then cut to full silence. Then, in your announcer voice:冷开场：广播先放很大声的电波杂音（10秒），突然切到完全安静。然后，用播音员的声音说：

"Good evening, listeners. This is Radio 1926 FM… or it was. When the clock struck midnight, the voice that never stopped talking — finally stopped. DJ Maxwell 'Mad Max' Loh: found in his studio chair. Headphones on. Microphone still on. Broadcasting… nothing. Dead. Air."“各位听众，晚上好。这里是 Radio 1926 FM……或者说，曾经是。半夜十二点钟声一响，那把从来不会停的声音——终于停了。DJ Maxwell 'Mad Max' Loh：被发现坐在他的播音椅上。耳机还戴着。麦克风还开着。播出去的……什么都没有。Dead. Air.——死掉的电波。”
2

Set the scene: "Six staff members were in the building that night. Every one of them had a reason. The police are forty minutes away. You — are already here." Introduce the three questions: WHO. HOW. WHERE. Hold up the notebook and show how to cross things off.铺垫：“那天晚上，有六个员工在大楼里。每一个，都有理由下手。警察还要四十分钟才到。而你们——现在就在这里。”介绍三大问题：是谁（WHO）。怎么下手（HOW）。在哪里（WHERE）。举起侦探笔记本，教大家怎么划格子排除。

3

Rules: read the 7 player rules from the Run Sheet word for word. Send each team to a different starting room. Start the clock. "The station is yours, detectives. Mind the static."规则：照着 Run Sheet 一字一字读出7条营员规则。把各小队分去不同的起点房间。开始计时。“这座电台交给你们了，侦探们。小心那片杂音。”

PA announcements during play游戏进行中的广播播报

  • Someone guessed wrong (HQ radios you):有人指认错了（HQ 用无线电报给你）： "Attention all units. Team [N] accused the killer… and got it WRONG. The killer is still out there. Team [N] cannot guess again for fifteen minutes. Carry on."“全体注意。第[N]小队指认了凶手……结果——错了。凶手还逍遥法外。第[N]小队十五分钟内不能再猜。请继续。”
  • Time calls: at 1:00 ("the police are halfway here"), 1:45 ("fifteen minutes until the final window"), 2:15 ("FINAL WINDOW — every team to HQ").报时：1:00（“警察的路已经走了一半”）、1:45（“距离最后答题时间还有十五分钟”）、2:15（“最后答题时间到——所有小队来 HQ 集合”）。
  • Someone got it right: big sound-effect moment — plan it with HQ so your announcement comes right after the terminal's win sound.有人猜中：大音效时刻——跟 HQ 讲好，让你的宣布刚好接在终端音效后面。

Reveal script (2:35–3:00)真相揭晓剧本（2:35–3:00）

1

Go through the giant cross-off grid clue by clue — cross off in the same order the teams did: no wounds (card 1), the dead mic and Stella's tape (card 2), the dust (card 3), the telegraph receipt (card 4), the sleeping jingle-man (card 5), the locked storage room (card 6), the lawyer's slip (card 7). End on: Tan. Coffee. Control Room.在巨型排除表上一条一条讲线索——照各小队破案的顺序划掉：伤痕（1号线索卡）、坏掉的麦克风和 Stella 的录音带（2号卡）、灰尘（3号卡）、电报收据（4号卡）、睡死的广告歌作者（5号卡）、锁住的储藏室（6号卡）、律师的预约单（7号卡）。最后落在：Tan。毒咖啡。Control Room。

2

Tan's confession (Tan acts it out, you tell the story around it): the shame, the thermos, Max storming into the Control Room, the drag marks, the static. Keep it to 60–90 seconds.Tan 的自白（Tan 负责演，你在旁边旁白）：那份屈辱、那个保温瓶、Max 冲进 Control Room、拖过的痕迹、那片杂音。控制在60–90秒。

3

Winners: first prize goes to the first team that accused correctly, then rank the final-window answers by how close they were (HQ hands you the terminal ranking). Prizes.胜出者：先颁给第一个指认凶手指对的小队，再照最后答题时间的准确度排名（HQ 会把终端排名给你）。颁奖。

4

Wrap-up (2 minutes — make the point, then stop):主题总结（2分钟，讲到位就收）：

"Every suspect had a motive. Every rumor sounded true. The room was FULL of noise — and the answer was never in the noise. You solved this by turning the noise off, piece by piece, until one true signal was left. That's this whole camp in one game: unplug from the noise… sync with the Spirit."“每个嫌疑人都有动机。每个流言听起来都像真的。整个房间都是噪音——但答案，从来不在噪音里。你们破得了这个案，是因为你们把噪音一个一个关掉，直到只剩下一个真的信号。整个营会，其实就浓缩在这个游戏里：关掉噪音……对准圣灵的频率。”

Read one verse (suggestion: 1 Kings 19:11–12, the still small voice). Pray. Done.读一节经文（建议：列王纪上19:11–12，那微小的声音）。祷告。收工。

  • Giant cross-off grid + marker for the reveal巨型排除表 + 真相揭晓用的马克笔
  • Printed scripts (this page) — don't make up your own wrap-up; the timing is planned打印好的剧本（就是这一页）——主题总结不要即兴发挥，时间是算好的
