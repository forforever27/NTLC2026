Floater — timing, traffic, repairs, rescue

You are the invisible stage manager. No costume needed (or a "STAFF" lanyard). If the game runs smoothly, nobody notices you existed. That's success.

Your loop (repeat every ~15 min)

1

Walk all 7 rooms. Check: room master okay? Props intact? Any team over 12 minutes in a room? (Nudge the room master to close it out.)

2

Spot idle or lost teams; point them to an empty room. At the 1-hour mark, collect cleared-room counts and report to HQ — any team under 2 clears gets personally shepherded.

3

Report room-clear counts to the HQ scoreboard. Take photos while you're at it (you're also the documentation crew).
