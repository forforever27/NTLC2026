Your round (walk it about every 15 min)你的巡逻路线（大约每15分钟走一轮）

1

Walk through all 7 rooms. Check: is the room master okay? Are the props all fine? Is any team stuck in one room for over 12 minutes? (If so, remind the room master to wrap it up.)走完全部7个房间。看看：关主还撑得住吗？道具还好好的吗？有没有小队在同一个房间待超过12分钟？（有就提醒关主收尾。）

2

Look for teams that are lost or have nothing to do; point them to an empty room. At the 1-hour mark, collect each team's cleared-room count and report to HQ — walk with any team that has cleared fewer than 2 rooms and guide them yourself.看到没事做或迷路的小队，指他们去空着的房间。到1小时的时候，收各队的通关数报给 HQ——通关不到2间的小队，要亲自带他们走。

3

Report room-clear counts to the HQ scoreboard. Take photos as you go (you're also the photo crew).把各房间的通关数报给 HQ 计分板。顺便拍照（你也是记录组）。
