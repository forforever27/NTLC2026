Floater — timing, traffic, repairs, rescue机动组——计时、疏导、维修、救场

You are the invisible stage manager. No costume needed (or a "STAFF" lanyard). If the game runs smoothly, nobody notices you existed. That's success.你是隐形的舞台监督。不需要戏服（或挂一条“STAFF”工作证）。如果游戏顺利进行，没人会注意到你的存在。那就是成功。

Your loop (repeat every ~15 min)你的巡逻循环（每约15分钟重复一次）

1

Walk all 7 rooms. Check: room master okay? Props intact? Any team over 12 minutes in a room? (Nudge the room master to close it out.)走遍全部7个房间。检查：关主状态还好吗？道具完好吗？有没有小队在同一个房间超过12分钟？（提醒关主收尾。）

2

Spot idle or lost teams; point them to an empty room. At the 1-hour mark, collect cleared-room counts and report to HQ — any team under 2 clears gets personally shepherded.留意闲着或迷路的小队；把他们指去空闲的房间。到1小时节点，收集各队通关数并汇报 HQ——通关少于2间的小队要亲自带路护送。

3

Report room-clear counts to the HQ scoreboard. Take photos while you're at it (you're also the documentation crew).向 HQ 计分板汇报各房间通关数。顺路拍照（你同时也是记录组）。
