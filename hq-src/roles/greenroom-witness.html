The Silent Witness — janitor with laryngitisThe Silent Witness（沉默证人）——失声的清洁工

Your character你的角色

The station janitor. You saw useful things that night — but you have total laryngitis (doctor's note pinned to your shirt). You never speak, not once, the entire game. Everything is mime, gestures, and mouthing words. You are not a suspect — you're a witness.电台的清洁工。那晚你看到了一些有用的事——但你完全失声了（医生证明就别在衣服上）。整场游戏你一句话都不说，一个字都不行。全靠比手画脚、手势和无声口型。你不是嫌疑人——你是证人。

Costume服装

Scarf around throat, doctor's note pinned on, mop or cleaning cloth.脖子围围巾，别上医生证明，拿一支拖把或抹布。

Your room你的房间

Cozy corner: couch/chairs (Joe "slept" here), snack wrappers, a thermos on a side table (subtle coffee nudge — never point at it).温馨小角落：沙发/椅子（Joe就是在这里"睡觉"的）、零食包装袋、边桌上放一个保温瓶（悄悄暗示咖啡——但绝对不要指它）。

Your mini-game — "The Silent Witness"你的小游戏——"The Silent Witness"（沉默证人） HANDMADE

1

Team enters; you point at the doctor's note, shrug apologetically. Your story must be extracted by charades / lip-reading / yes-no nodding.小队进来时，你指一指医生证明，抱歉地耸耸肩。他们只能靠比手画脚、读唇、点头摇头来把你的故事"问"出来。

2

The story you mime: (a) man with guitar (strum gesture) lay on couch at 11 (point at clock, then couch, mime snoring); (b) he never left (walk fingers, cross arms NO); (c) you also never left this room (point at self, at floor, shake head).你要比出来的故事：（a）一个抱吉他的男人（做弹吉他手势）11点躺在沙发上（指时钟，再指沙发，比打呼噜）；（b）他一直没离开过（手指做走路状，双臂交叉表示"没有"）；（c）你自己也没离开过这个房间（指自己，指地板，摇头）。

3

The team must repeat the full story back to you correctly (all 3 beats). Nod solemnly → hand Clue Card 5 — Half A plus one paper token. Mime: "other half… out there… another team" (point at token, point at door, hold up two fingers, interlock hands).小队必须把完整的故事（3个要点）正确复述给你。你郑重地点头 → 递上线索卡5——上半张（Half A）和一枚纸信物。再比："另一半……在外面……要找另一队"（指信物，指门口，竖起两根手指，双手十指相扣）。

Why the split clue matters: Half B is only obtainable at the HQ desk by two token-holding teams meeting and presenting tokens together. You cannot explain this verbally — that's the fun. If a team truly doesn't get it after your mime, point at the token and mouth "H-Q" slowly.为什么拆分线索很重要：下半张（Half B）只能在HQ服务台领取——必须由两支各持信物的小队会合、一起出示信物才行。你不能用嘴解释——这正是乐趣所在。如果比了半天小队还是不懂，就指着信物，用口型慢慢地说"H-Q"。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Everything above, in mime only.以上所有内容——但只能用比的。
  • NEVER speak. Not to answer rules questions, not when laughed at, not after your shift. Breaking silence breaks the room.绝对不说话。被问规则不说，被笑也不说，下班了还是不说。一开口，这个房间就"破功"了。
  • You saw nothing about the murder itself — if teams push (mime "did you see the killer?"), big sad shrug, shake head.关于凶案本身你什么都没看到——如果小队追问（比划"你看到凶手了吗？"），就无奈地大大耸肩，摇头。
  • Never gesture toward the thermos. It sits there. That's all.永远不要指向保温瓶。它就摆在那儿。仅此而已。
