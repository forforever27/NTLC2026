Station 5 · Green Room第5站 · Green Room（休息室）

The Silent Witness — the janitor who lost his voiceThe Silent Witness（沉默证人）——失声的清洁工

Your character你的角色

You are the station janitor. You saw useful things that night — but you have laryngitis (you lost your voice completely; the doctor's note is pinned to your shirt). You never speak. Not one word, the whole game. You only mime, use gestures, and mouth words. You are not a suspect — you are a witness.你是电台的清洁工。那晚你看到了一些有用的东西——可是你完全失声，讲不出话（医生证明就别在衣服上）。整场游戏你一句话都不能讲，一个字都不行。全部靠比手画脚、做手势、用口型。你不是嫌疑人——你是证人。

Costume服装

Scarf around your neck, doctor's note pinned on, a mop or cleaning cloth.脖子围围巾，别上医生证明，拿一支拖把或抹布。

Your room你的房间

Set up a cozy corner: a couch or chairs (Joe "slept" here), snack wrappers, and a thermos on a side table (a quiet coffee hint — but never point at it).布置一个舒服的小角落：沙发/椅子（Joe就是在这里"睡觉"的）、零食包装袋、小桌上放一个保温瓶（偷偷暗示咖啡——但千万不要去指它）。

Your mini-game — "The Silent Witness" (two parts)你的小游戏——"The Silent Witness"（沉默证人）（分两关） HANDMADE

1

When the team comes in, point at the doctor's note and give a sorry shrug. Then hold up the deck of scene cards and wave them over: before you "talk", THEY must act for YOU.小队进来时，你指一指医生证明，不好意思地耸耸肩。然后举起那叠表演卡，向他们招手：想让你"开口"？先演给你看。

2

Part 1 — "The Audition" (about 4 min). The team draws 3 scene cards. For each card: one or two players act it out with no sound. The REST of the team must guess it (the guessers may talk — only you can't). You are the judge: thumbs up = pass, thumbs down = try again (give 30 more seconds, then let it pass). All 3 done → clap silently and give a big welcome gesture. Now they have earned your story — and they have learned to read mime, which they need for Part 2.第一关——"试镜"（大约4分钟）。小队抽3张表演卡。每张卡：派一到两个人不出声地演，其他队员负责猜（猜的人可以讲话——只有你不能）。你来评分：竖拇指＝过，倒拇指＝再来一次（多给30秒，然后放水让过）。3张全过 → 你无声地鼓掌，做一个大大的欢迎手势。现在他们才有资格听你的故事——而且刚好练会了"看比手画脚"，第二关正需要。

3

Part 2 — the story you mime: (a) a man holding a guitar (do a strumming move) lay on the couch at 11 (point at the clock, then the couch, then mime snoring); (b) he never left (walk your fingers, then cross your arms for NO); (c) you also never left this room (point at yourself, then the floor, then shake your head).第二关——你要比出来的证词：（a）一个抱吉他的男人（做弹吉他手势）11点躺在沙发上（指时钟，再指沙发，比打呼噜）；（b）他一直没离开过（手指做走路状，双臂交叉表示"没有"）；（c）你自己也没离开过这个房间（指自己，指地板，摇头）。

4

The team must tell the full story back to you correctly (all 3 parts). Nod seriously → hand over Clue Card 5 — Half A plus one paper token. Then mime: "other half… out there… another team" (point at the token, point at the door, hold up two fingers, lock your hands together).小队要把整个故事（3个要点）讲回给你听，全部讲对才算过。你郑重地点头 → 递上线索卡5——上半张（Half A）和一张配对小卡（信物）。再比："另一半……在外面……要找另一队"（指配对小卡，指门口，竖起两根手指，双手十指相扣）。

Timing: the two parts together take 8–10 minutes, same as the other rooms. If a team is way ahead of everyone, be a stricter judge in Part 1. If they are running late, pass them faster.时间：两关加起来8–10分钟，跟其他房间一样。如果某队进度快过头，第一关就当严一点的评审；如果他们落后了，就快点放行。

Why the half-and-half clue matters: Half B can only be picked up at the HQ desk — two teams, each holding a token, must show up together and show their tokens at the same time. You cannot explain this with words — that's the fun part. If a team still doesn't get it after your mime, point at the token and mouth "H-Q" slowly.为什么这个一半一半的线索很重要：下半张（Half B）只能去HQ服务台拿——要两支小队各拿着自己的配对小卡，一起到场、一起出示才拿得到。你不可以开口解释——好玩就好玩在这里。如果比了半天小队还是不懂，就指着配对小卡，用口型慢慢说"H-Q"。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Everything above, in mime only.上面写的全部——但只能用比的。
  • NEVER speak. Not to explain the rules, not when people laugh at you, not even after your shift. One word and the whole room is ruined.绝对不能讲话。被问规则不讲，被笑也不讲，轮完班还是不讲。一开口，这个房间就"破功"了。
  • You saw nothing of the murder itself. If teams keep pushing (miming "did you see the killer?"), give a big sad shrug and shake your head.凶案本身你什么都没看到——如果小队一直追问（比"你看到凶手了吗？"），就很无奈地大大耸肩，摇头。
  • Never point at the thermos. Just let it sit there. That's all.千万不要去指保温瓶。让它摆在那边就好。这样就够了。

Q&A (mimed)问答（全程比手画脚）

If they ask…如果他们问……You mime…你就比……
"Can you write it down instead?""那你可以写下来吗？"Hold up your hands — mime writing, then wince and shake your head, and point at the doctor's note (doctor's orders: full rest — even your hands, it seems. It's a joke moment, but don't give in)举起双手——比写字的动作，然后皱眉摇头，指医生证明（医生说要完全休息，看来连手也要休息——这是搞笑位，但你要装到底）
"Who do you think did it?""你觉得是谁干的？"Look left, look right, lean in close… then give a huge shrug左看看，右看看，凑过去……然后超夸张地大耸肩
"Is this thermos important?""这个保温瓶重要吗？"Pick it up, take a happy sip, put it back (it's just your coffee — the best fake clue ever)拿起来，很享受地喝一口，放回去（那只是你的咖啡——最厉害的假线索）
