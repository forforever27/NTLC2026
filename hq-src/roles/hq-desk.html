Crew · Detective's Office筹委 · 侦探办公室

HQ Desk — accusations, hints, tokens, scoreboard总部侦探台——正式指控、提示、信物、计分板

Two people. You are the referees. Neutral, procedural, slightly noir — think "precinct desk sergeant". You are NOT suspects and know the full solution; your poker face is the most important costume in the game.两个人。你们是裁判。中立、照章办事、带点黑色电影味——想象“警局前台的值班警长”。你们不是嫌疑人，并且知道完整答案；你们的扑克脸是全场最重要的戏服。

Your four jobs你们的四项任务

1 · Accusation Terminal1 · 指控终端

Laptop runs /props/accuse/. On game day, open the GM setup screen (gear icon), set the answer (Tuner Tan / Poisoned coffee / Control Room) and the open-time (0:45). Teams select who/how/where and submit. The app enforces the 15-min lockout, logs every attempt, and plays the sting on a win. Radio the emcee after every attempt so it's announced.笔记本电脑运行 /props/accuse/。游戏当天，打开 GM 设置界面（齿轮图标），设定答案（Tuner Tan／毒咖啡／Control Room）和开放时间（0:45）。各小队自行选择嫌疑人／作案手法／案发地点并提交。程序会自动执行15分钟禁赛、记录每次尝试，并在猜中时播放音效。每次指控后用无线电通知司仪进行广播。

2 · Hint sales2 · 提示购买

Any team may buy a duplicate of any room's clue card for a 10-minute wait. Take their team number, note the time, hand the card when time's up: "Your film has developed, detective." Keep a full spare set of all 7 clue cards.任何小队都可以用等待10分钟为代价，购买任意房间线索卡的副本。记下队号、写下时间，时间一到把卡递上：“你的底片洗出来了，侦探。”手边常备一整套7张线索卡的备用卡。

3 · Split-clue tokens3 · 拆分线索信物

You hold the Half B cards. When TWO teams each present a Green Room token together at your desk, both teams receive Half B. One team alone: "Regulations. Two witnesses to unseal testimony. Find another token holder."你们保管 Half B 卡（证词下半份）。当两支小队同时在台前各出示一枚 Green Room 信物时，两队都可领取 Half B。只来一队时说：“按规定，开封证词需要两名见证人。去找另一位信物持有者吧。”

4 · Scoreboard4 · 计分板

Whiteboard: rooms cleared per team (room masters radio you or floater relays), accusations made (✗/✓), hints bought. Public info only — never the content of clues.白板记录：各小队已通关的房间（由关主无线电通报，或机动组转达）、指控记录（✗/✓）、已购提示。只公开这些信息——绝不公开线索内容。

Procedures流程

  • Accusation flow: whole team present → confirm "this is a formal accusation" → they enter it in the terminal themselves → read result deadpan → radio emcee.指控流程：全队到场 → 确认“这是一次正式指控” → 由他们自己在终端输入 → 面无表情地读出结果 → 无线电通知司仪。
  • Final window (2:15): switch the terminal to Final Window mode (GM screen) — lockouts off, every team submits once. The terminal ranks by accuracy then time.最终提交窗口（2:15）：把终端切换到 Final Window 模式（GM 界面）——解除禁赛，每队提交一次。终端先按准确度、再按时间排名。
  • Disputes: you decide, emcee announces, no appeals. Rule of thumb: generosity on mini-game judging, zero flexibility on accusation rules.争议：你们裁决，司仪宣布，不得上诉。原则：小游戏判定从宽，指控规则寸步不让。

Lines you may enjoy你可以尽情享用的台词

"An accusation is a serious instrument, detective. The city remembers the careless."“指控是一件严肃的工具，侦探。这座城市，记得每一个草率的人。”
"Wrong. The killer is still out there — and now they know you're guessing." (after a failed accusation)“错。凶手还在外面——而且现在，他们知道你是在瞎猜了。”（指控失败后）
"Two witnesses to unseal testimony. Regulations."“开封证词，需要两名见证人。规定就是规定。”

Never do绝对不要

  • React to near-miss accusations ("ooh, so close") — deadpan only.对差一点就对的指控做出反应（“哦，好接近”）——只准面无表情。
  • Reveal how many parts of a failed accusation were right. The terminal says only WRONG. That's the design.透露失败的指控中猜对了几项。终端只显示 WRONG（错误）。这是刻意的设计。
  • Let teams hover near the terminal while another team accuses.让其他小队在别队指控时于终端附近逗留围观。

Props you prepare你要准备的道具

  • Laptop with terminal loaded, GM answer set, tested (do a fake run the night before)装好终端程序的笔记本电脑，设好 GM 答案，测试完毕（前一晚做一次彩排）
  • Whiteboard + markers, timer/phone, spare full clue-card set, Half B cards ×8, token match-list白板+马克笔、计时器／手机、整套备用线索卡、Half B 卡×8、信物配对表
  • Paper accusation slips (fallback if laptop dies) + the answer in a sealed envelope纸质指控单（笔记本电脑罢工时的后备方案）+ 装在密封信封里的答案
