Crew · Detective's Office筹委 · 侦探办公室

HQ Desk — accusing the killer, hints, tokens, scoreboard总部侦探台——指认凶手（正式指控）、提示、配对小卡（信物）、计分板

Two people. You are the referees. Stay neutral, go by the rules, keep a bit of that old detective-movie feel — think "desk sergeant at a police station". You are NOT suspects, and you know the full answer. Your poker face is the most important costume in the game.两个人。你们是裁判。中立、照规矩来、带一点黑色电影的味道——想象“警局前台的值班警长”。你们不是嫌疑人，而且知道全部答案；你们的扑克脸是全场最重要的戏服。

Your four jobs你们要做的四件事

1 · Accusation Terminal1 · 指认凶手终端

The laptop runs /props/accuse/. On game day, open the GM setup screen (gear icon), set the answer (Tuner Tan / Poisoned coffee / Control Room) and the open time (0:45). Teams pick who / how / where and submit it themselves. The app blocks a team from guessing for 15 minutes after a wrong guess, records every try, and plays the win sound when someone gets it right. Radio the emcee after every try so it gets announced.笔记本电脑跑 /props/accuse/。游戏当天，打开 GM 设置画面（齿轮图标），设好答案（Tuner Tan／毒咖啡／Control Room）和开放时间（0:45）。小队自己选：是谁、用什么方法杀的、在哪里，然后交上去。程序会自动执行“猜错 15 分钟内不能再猜”、记录每一次尝试，猜中时还会播音效。每次有人指认，就用无线电通知司仪广播出去。

2 · Hint sales2 · 卖提示

Any team can buy a copy of any room's clue card. The price: a 10-minute wait. Write down their team number and the time, and hand over the card when the time is up: "Your film has developed, detective." Keep a full spare set of all 7 clue cards.任何小队都可以“买”任何房间线索卡的副本，代价是等 10 分钟。记下队号和时间，时间到就把卡递过去：“你的底片洗好了，侦探。”手边随时放一整套 7 张备用线索卡。

3 · Split-clue tokens3 · 一半一半的线索

You hold the Half B cards. When TWO teams come to your desk together and each shows a Green Room token, both teams get Half B. If one team comes alone: "Rules are rules. It takes two witnesses to open a sealed statement. Go find another token holder."Half B 卡（证词的后半张）在你们手上。两支小队同时在台前各拿出一张 Green Room 配对小卡，两队才可以拿 Half B。只来一队就说：“照规定，拆证词要两个见证人。去找另一个有小卡的人吧。”

4 · Scoreboard4 · 计分板

Whiteboard: rooms cleared by each team (room masters radio you, or the floater passes it on), accuse attempts (✗/✓), hints bought. Public info only — never what is written on the clues.白板写：各队通关了哪些房间（关主用无线电报给你们，或机动组转达）、指认记录（✗/✓）、买过的提示。只公开这些——线索内容绝对不可以公开。

Procedures流程

  • When a team comes to accuse (say who + how + where): the whole team must be there → confirm "so you are accusing the killer now?" → they type it into the terminal themselves → read the result with a straight face → radio the emcee.指认流程：全队都要到 → 跟他们确认“你们这是要指认凶手了哦” → 让他们自己在终端输入 → 面无表情地读出结果 → 无线电通知司仪。
  • Final window (2:15): switch the terminal to Final Window mode (GM screen) — the "no guessing" block is off, every team submits once. The terminal ranks teams by how close they are, then by time.最后答题时间（2:15）：把终端切去 Final Window 模式（GM 画面）——“不能再猜”的限制解除，每队交一次答案。终端会先看准确度、再看时间来排名。
  • If there is an argument: you decide, the emcee announces it, and that is final. Rule of thumb: be kind when judging mini-games, but never bend the rules about accusing.有争议怎么办：你们决定，司仪宣布，不可以上诉。原则：小游戏判松一点没关系，指认规则一步都不让。

Lines you can use这些台词随便你用

"Accusing someone is a serious thing, detective. This city remembers the careless."“指认凶手是很严肃的事，侦探。这座城市，记得每一个乱来的人。”
"Wrong. The killer is still out there — and now they know you're guessing." (use after a wrong guess)“错。凶手还在外面——而且现在，他们知道你们是在乱猜了。”（指认错之后用）
"Two witnesses to open a sealed statement. Rules are rules."“拆证词，要两个见证人。规定就是规定。”

Never do绝对不要

  • React when a guess is almost right ("ooh, so close") — straight face only.对差一点就中的指认有反应（“哇，好近”）——只准面无表情。
  • Tell a team how many parts of their wrong guess were right. The terminal only says WRONG. That is on purpose.告诉人家猜错的那次里对了几项。终端只会显示 WRONG（错）。这是故意设计的。
  • Let other teams stand near the terminal while a team is accusing.让别的小队在有人指认时挤在终端旁边看。

Props you prepare你要准备的道具

  • Laptop with the terminal loaded, GM answer set, tested (do a practice run the night before)装好终端程序的笔记本电脑，GM 答案设好，测试过（前一晚先假装跑一次）
  • Whiteboard + markers, timer/phone, a full spare set of clue cards, Half B cards ×8, token match-list白板+马克笔、计时器／手机、一整套备用线索卡、Half B 卡×8、配对小卡对照表
  • Paper accuse slips (backup if the laptop dies) + the answer in a sealed envelope纸本指认单（笔记本电脑罢工时的后备）+ 装在密封信封里的答案
