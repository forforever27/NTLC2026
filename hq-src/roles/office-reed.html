Mr. Reed — the blackmailed managerMr. Reed——被勒索的经理

Your character你的角色

A smooth talker who has been backed into a corner. Max was blackmailing you over faked ratings, and everyone half-knows it. On paper you look the most guilty — motive, money, power. But you are innocent: at midnight you were downtown with your lawyer, signing the settlement (a legal deal), and the Intern drove you there.讲话滑溜溜、但被逼到墙角的经理。Max抓着你造假收听率的把柄跟你要钱，这件事大家多多少少都听说过。表面上看你嫌疑最大——有动机、有钱、有权。但你是清白的：半夜十二点你人在市区，跟律师签和解协议，是The Intern开车载你去的。

Costume服装

Blazer, a loose tie, and an expensive-looking pen you click when you're nervous.西装外套、领带松松地挂着、一支看起来很贵的笔——一紧张就按它。

Your room你的房间

A desk piled with papers. Hidden in the pile are the three lock-code puzzle props: a wall calendar with one date circled (7), a ratings chart where one bar is clearly faked — the fake month's number is (2), and the lawyer's memo giving the last two digits: the last digit of 1926 (6) and the keys on the sketched keyring (3). Scatter the decoy sticky notes too — every number on them is a dead end. The locked box (4-digit code 7263) sits in plain sight on the desk with Clue Card 7 inside.堆满文件的办公桌，里面藏着密码锁谜题的三样道具：圈了一个日期的挂历（7）、有一根柱明显造假的收听率图表——假的那个月的数字是（2）、律师的便条，给出最后两位数：1926 的最后一位（6）和便条上钥匙圈的钥匙数（3）。混淆用的便利贴也要散在桌上——上面每个数字都是死路。上锁的盒子（四位数密码7263）就摆在桌上很显眼的地方，里面装着线索卡7。

Your mini-game — "The Locked Drawer"你的小游戏——"The Locked Drawer"（上锁的抽屉） HANDMADE

1

Say: "My private records are in that box. If it clears my name, fine — open it. The code? If I could remember codes, I wouldn't need a lawyer. It's all… somewhere in this paperwork." Wave vaguely at the desk.开场说明："我的私人记录都在那个盒子里。要是能还我清白，行——打开吧。密码？我要是记得住密码，还请律师做什么。反正……都在这堆文件里的某个地方。"朝办公桌随便挥挥手。

2

The team hunts for the four digits across the three real props (the sticky notes lead nowhere). You confirm nothing. At most, raise an eyebrow when they hold the right prop.小队要在三样真道具里找出四个数字（便利贴全是死路）。你什么都不帮他们确认；他们拿对道具的时候，你最多挑一下眉毛。

3

Two wrong codes in a row → 1-minute time-out ("The lock is jammed. Cheap lock. Everything in this station is cheap."). Box opens → they take Clue Card 7. Stuck >10 min → give a hint: "Calendar. Chart. Memo. In that order, maybe."连续输错两次密码 → 暂停1分钟（"锁卡住了。便宜货。这电台里就没有一样东西不便宜。"）。打开盒子 → 他们拿走线索卡7。卡超过10分钟 → 给提示："挂历。图表。便条。也许，就照这个顺序。"

Your lines你的台词

"Whatever Max said about the ratings is a lie. Where was I at midnight? …Ask my lawyer. I mean it — really, go ask my lawyer.""Max讲的什么收听率的事，全是乱讲。半夜十二点我在哪里？……去问我的律师。我是认真的——真的，去问我的律师。"
"Yes, I paid him. Businessmen pay people to stay quiet all the time. It's cheaper than the other option — wait, that came out wrong.""对，我有给他钱。做生意的花钱买人闭嘴，很平常。总比另一种办法便宜——呃，这句好像讲错了。"
(if accused) "I fix problems with lawyers, not with… whatever this was. Check the box."（若被指控）"我解决问题都是靠律师，不是……不管这案子是用什么。去看盒子里的东西。"

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Your alibi: a meeting with your lawyer + the Intern drove you (this is exactly what Clue Card 7 proves — let the card do the talking).你的不在场证明：约了律师+The Intern开车载你（线索卡7证明的就是这件事——让卡片自己讲话）。
  • If asked about the Intern: "Jumpy kid. Useful. He was with me all night — too bad for your theory."如果被问到The Intern："毛毛躁躁的小子。蛮好用的。整晚都跟着我——不好意思咯，你的推理不成立。"
  • You don't know who did it. If they keep pushing: "In my experience, it's always whoever the paperwork says it is."你不知道是谁干的。被一直逼问就说："照我的经验，文件写的是谁，凶手就是谁。"
  • Never confirm if a digit is right or a prop matters. An eyebrow raise is the most you give.绝对不要帮他们确认数字对不对、道具有没有用。最多挑一下眉毛。
