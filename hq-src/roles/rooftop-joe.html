Station 6 · Rooftop Antenna第6站 · Rooftop Antenna（天台天线）

Jingle Joe — the man who speaks in jinglesJingle Joe——说话都带广告歌的男人

Your character你的角色

A kind but broke jingle writer. Max made fun of your songs on air, and the station never paid your royalties (the money they owed you for your songs). Your way of coping: turn everything into a jingle — every single thing you say comes out half-sung. You are innocent: you slept the whole night on the Green Room couch.人很好但很穷的广告歌作者。Max在直播里笑过你的歌，电台也一直欠你版税没给。你的办法就是把所有事都变成广告歌——你讲的每一句话都会忍不住半唱出来。你是清白的：那一整晚你都在Green Room的沙发上呼呼大睡。

Costume服装

Ukulele/guitar/kazoo (or play air guitar like you mean it). A loud, flashy shirt.尤克里里/吉他/卡祖笛（或者很投入地弹空气吉他也行）。一件很花的衬衫。

Your room你的房间

The "rooftop": an antenna made from cardboard or a broom + foil, string as "cables", and the storage inventory sheet on the wall (your clue comes from this sheet). The relay game needs a stretch where both ends can see each other — a corridor, two ends of a hall, or an outdoor space."天台"：用纸皮/扫把+锡纸做的天线、绳子当"电缆"、墙上贴着仓库物品清单（你的线索就是从这张清单来的）。接力游戏需要一段两边能互相看到的距离——一条走廊、大厅的两端，或一段户外空地。

Your mini-game — "Signal Relay"你的小游戏——"Signal Relay"（信号接力） HANDMADE optional app

1

Explain the game (by singing, of course): "♪ The antenna's dead, the phones are down, the only signal left in town… is LIGHT! ♪" Split the team in half: half are Senders (at the far end, with a word card + Morse chart + phone flashlight), half are Receivers (at your end, with a Morse chart + pen). No talking or hand signs across the gap.开场说明（当然是唱的）："♪ 天线断了线，电话没了电，全城只剩一种信号……是光线！♪" 把小队分成两半：一半当发送员（站远的那端，拿单词卡+摩斯密码表+手机手电筒），一半当接收员（站你这端，拿摩斯密码表+笔）。隔着这段距离不可以讲话、不可以打手势。

2

Senders send the word with flashlight blinks (short/long). Receivers use the Morse chart to work it out and write it down. Word 1: STATIC. Then swap sides. Word 2: ANTENNA.发送员用手电筒闪灯（短闪/长闪）把单词发过去。接收员对着密码表翻译出来，写下来。第一个词：STATIC。然后两边交换。第二个词：ANTENNA。

3

Both words correct → hand over Clue Card 6. Optional: receivers can check their answer with the Morse Checker app at /props/morse/. The app shows the finish code SIGNAL-6, which they must tell you. Stuck >10 min → change word 2 to the shorter "SOS".两个词都对 → 递上线索卡6。可选：接收员可以用 /props/morse/ 的摩斯检查小程序对答案，程序会跳出完成码 SIGNAL-6，他们要把这个码报给你听。卡超过10分钟 → 第二个词换成短一点的"SOS"。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Your alibi: you were asleep in the Green Room all night (the witness can back you up — send teams there).你的不在场证明：整晚都睡在Green Room（那边的证人可以帮你作证——叫小队去找他）。
  • You know the storage inventory sheet was signed at 9 pm — "all the heavy gear was locked away, I watched them do it" (this matches your own clue).你知道仓库清单是晚上9点签名确认的——"所有大件器材都锁起来了，我亲眼看着他们锁的"（这刚好对得上你自己的线索）。
  • You don't know who did it. If asked, dodge the question with a song.你不知道是谁干的。被问到就用唱歌把话题带开。
  • Don't explain what SIGNAL-6 or the words mean — they are just test words for the signal.不要讲 SIGNAL-6 或那两个词是什么意思——它们只是传信号的测试词而已。
