Jingle Joe — the man who speaks in jinglesJingle Joe——说话都带广告歌的男人

Your character你的角色

Sweet, broke jingle writer. Max mocked your songs on air and the station never paid your royalties. You cope by turning everything into a jingle — literally everything you say comes out half-sung. You're innocent: you slept through the whole night on the Green Room couch.善良又穷困的广告歌作者。Max在直播里嘲笑过你的歌，电台也一直没付你版税。你的应对方式就是把一切都变成广告歌——你说的每一句话都会不由自主地半唱出来。你是清白的：那一整晚你都在Green Room的沙发上呼呼大睡。

Costume服装

Ukulele/guitar/kazoo (or air-guitar committedly). Loud shirt.尤克里里/吉他/卡祖笛（或者全情投入地弹空气吉他）。一件花哨的衬衫。

Your room你的房间

"Rooftop": antenna made of cardboard/broom + foil, string "cables", the storage inventory sheet posted (it's the basis of your clue). Needs a sight-gap for the relay — a corridor, two ends of a hall, or an outdoor stretch."天台"：用纸皮/扫把+锡纸做的天线、绳子当"电缆"、墙上贴着仓库物品清单（这是你线索的依据）。接力游戏需要一段能互相看见的距离——一条走廊、大厅两端，或一段户外空地。

Your mini-game — "Signal Relay"你的小游戏——"Signal Relay"（信号接力） HANDMADE optional app

1

Brief (sung, obviously): "♪ The antenna's dead, the phones are down, the only signal left in town… is LIGHT! ♪" Split the team: half are Senders (far end, with a word card + Morse chart + phone flashlight), half are Receivers (your end, Morse chart + pen). No talking or gesturing across the gap.开场说明（当然是唱的）："♪ 天线断了线，电话没了电，全城只剩一种信号……是光线！♪" 把小队分成两半：一半当发送员（站远端，拿单词卡+摩斯密码表+手机手电筒），一半当接收员（站你这端，拿摩斯密码表+笔）。隔着这段距离不许说话、不许打手势。

2

Senders transmit the word by flashlight blinks (short/long). Receivers decode and write it down. Word 1: STATIC. Then swap roles. Word 2: ANTENNA.发送员用手电筒的闪光（短闪/长闪）把单词发过去。接收员解码并写下来。第一个词：STATIC。然后交换角色。第二个词：ANTENNA。

3

Both words correct → hand Clue Card 6. Optional: receivers verify via the Morse Checker app at /props/morse/, which shows completion code SIGNAL-6 they must tell you. Stuck >10 min → shorten word 2 to "SOS".两个词都对 → 递上线索卡6。可选：接收员用 /props/morse/ 的摩斯校验小程序核对，程序会显示完成码 SIGNAL-6，他们必须报给你听。卡关超过10分钟 → 把第二个词缩短为"SOS"。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • Your alibi: asleep in the Green Room all night (the witness can confirm — send teams there).你的不在场证明：整晚都睡在Green Room（证人可以证实——让小队去那边找他）。
  • You know the storage inventory was signed at 9 pm — "all the heavy gear was locked away, I watched them do it" (supports your own clue).你知道仓库清单是晚上9点签收的——"所有重型器材都锁起来了，我亲眼看着他们锁的"（这能佐证你自己的线索）。
  • You don't know who did it. Deflect musically.你不知道是谁干的。用音乐岔开话题。
  • Don't reveal what SIGNAL-6 or the words mean — they're just transmission tests.不要透露 SIGNAL-6 或那两个词的含义——它们只是发报测试而已。
