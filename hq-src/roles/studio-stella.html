"Static" Stella — rival DJ"Static" Stella —— 死对头 DJ

Your character你的角色

Glamorous, salty rival DJ whose show was cancelled to give Max a double slot. You resent him openly — you're the obvious suspect and you know it, which annoys you. You are innocent: you were live on Channel 2 from 11 pm to 1 am.光鲜亮丽、嘴上不饶人的死对头 DJ。你的节目被砍掉，就为了给 Mad Max 腾出双倍时段。你对他的不满摆在明面上——你是那个最明显的嫌疑人，你自己也清楚，这点让你很不爽。你是清白的：晚上 11 点到凌晨 1 点，你一直在 Channel 2 做直播。

Costume服装

Headphones around neck, scarf, sunglasses. Diva energy.耳机挂脖、丝巾、墨镜。全程大牌气场。

Your room你的房间

The crime scene: masking-tape body outline in the DJ chair area, caution tape, cracked vinyl record near the body (red herring — leave it obvious), ON AIR sign, cardboard mic.案发现场：DJ 椅一带用美纹胶带贴出人形轮廓，拉上警戒线，"尸体"旁放一张裂开的黑胶唱片（烟雾弹（假线索）——摆得越显眼越好），加上 ON AIR 灯牌和纸皮麦克风。

Your mini-game — "One Take"你的小游戏 —— "One Take"（一条过） HANDMADE

1

Hand the team the bulletin script (3 tongue-twisters inside). Brief: "Radio means ONE take. Everyone speaks at least one line. No laughing, no stumbles, 30 seconds."把新闻稿递给小队（里面藏了 3 段绕口令）。开场词："电台就是一条过。每个人至少念一句。不许笑场、不许吃螺丝，30 秒内完成。"

2

Record on your phone. Any stumble/laugh = restart the take. Play it back to them so they hear themselves — that's half the fun.用你的手机录音。只要有人吃螺丝或笑场，整条重来。录完放给他们听自己的声音——这才是乐趣的一半。

3

Max 3 failed takes → 4th attempt passes automatically (don't tell them that). Success → hand Clue Card 1.最多失败 3 次 → 第 4 次自动过关（别告诉他们）。成功后 → 发放线索卡 1。

Your lines (stay light, 2–3 uses each)你的台词（点到为止，每句用 2–3 次）

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • You know your own alibi (live on Channel 2). Freely point teams to "the broadcast log" (Control Room) to verify it.你清楚自己的不在场证明（在 Channel 2 直播）。可以大方地让小队去查"播出记录"（Control Room）核实。
  • You saw Max drinking coffee "like always" during the break — you may mention this if pressed hard (subtle nudge toward the method).你看到 Max 在休息时段"照常"喝咖啡——如果被追问得紧，可以提这一句（悄悄把他们往作案手法上引）。
  • You do NOT know who did it. Never speculate about Tan.你并不知道凶手是谁。绝不要猜测 Tuner Tan。
  • Never explain what your clue card means — let them read it.绝不解释线索卡的含义——让他们自己读。
