Your character你的角色

A stylish, sharp-tongued rival DJ. Your show got cancelled so Mad Max could get a double slot. You hate him and you don't hide it — so you're the obvious suspect, you know it, and it annoys you. But you are innocent: you were live on air on Channel 2 from 11 pm to 1 am.打扮到美美、讲话很呛的死对头 DJ。你的节目被砍掉，就为了让 Mad Max 拿双倍时段。你讨厌他，而且完全不遮掩——所以你是那个最明显的嫌疑人，你自己也知道，这点让你很不爽。但你是清白的：晚上 11 点到凌晨 1 点，你都在 Channel 2 做直播。

Costume服装

Headphones around neck, scarf, sunglasses. Diva energy.耳机挂脖、丝巾、墨镜。全程大牌气场。

Your room你的房间

This is the crime scene. Tape a body outline on the floor near the DJ chair, put up caution tape, place a cracked vinyl record next to the "body" (a fake clue — make it easy to spot), plus an ON AIR sign and a cardboard mic.命案现场：在 DJ 椅那边用美纹胶带贴一个人形轮廓，拉上警戒线，"尸体"旁边放一张裂开的黑胶唱片（假线索——摆到明显一点），再加 ON AIR 灯牌和纸皮麦克风。

Your mini-game — "One Take"你的小游戏 —— "One Take"（一条过） HANDMADE

1

Give the team the news script. It hides 3 tongue-twisters, plus the big finish: every player sings the Queen of the Night run ("Ah! ah-ah-ah-ah…") one at a time, as high as they dare. Say: "Radio means ONE take. Everyone reads a line, everyone sings the run. No laughing, no slip-ups."把新闻稿递给小队——里面藏了 3 段绕口令，还有压轴：每个人轮流唱一次"夜后"那句（"啊！啊啊啊啊——"），能飙多高飙多高。开场词："电台就是一条过。每个人念一句，每个人唱一次。不准笑场、不准吃螺丝。"

2

Record it on your phone. If anyone laughs or messes up, the whole take restarts. Play the recording back so they hear themselves — that's half the fun.用你的手机录音。只要有人吃螺丝或笑场，整条重来。录完放给他们听自己的声音——好笑的就是这个。

3

If they fail 3 takes, the 4th try passes automatically (don't tell them this). When they pass, give them Clue Card 1.最多失败 3 次 → 第 4 次自动过关（这个别讲给他们听）。成功后 → 发线索卡 1。

What you know / never reveal你知道的 / 绝不透露的

  • You have an alibi — proof you were somewhere else: you were live on Channel 2. Feel free to send teams to "the broadcast log" (Control Room) to check it.你有不在场证明（当时在 Channel 2 直播）。可以大大方方叫小队去 Control Room 查"播出记录"确认。
  • You saw Max drinking coffee "like always" during the break. Only mention this if they really push you (it's a quiet hint about how he was killed).你看到 Max 休息时段"照常"喝咖啡——如果他们一直追问，可以提这一句（偷偷引他们去想凶手是用什么方法杀的）。
  • You do NOT know who did it. Never guess about Tuner Tan.你不知道凶手是谁。千万别去猜 Tuner Tan。
  • Never explain what your clue card means — let them read it themselves.千万别解释线索卡写什么——让他们自己读。
