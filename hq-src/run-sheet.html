Rules briefed to players (emcee reads these)念给营员听的规则（司仪来念）

  1. Stay with your team. You need your whole team to play a room.全程跟着你的小队走。每个房间都要全队到齐才能玩。
  2. Only one team in a room at a time. If a room is taken, go to another one — there is always a free room.一个房间一次只能进一队。里面有人就去别间——一定有空房。
  3. Win the room's challenge to get its clue card. Each team can clear each room only once.赢了那个房间的挑战，才能拿到它的线索卡。每个房间每队只能过一次。
  4. You can ask the suspects anything, but you cannot force them. They will lie about only one thing: whether they are the killer.嫌疑人你们可以随便问，但不能逼他。他们只会骗你们一件事：自己是不是凶手。
  5. From 0:45, you can accuse the killer at the HQ desk: say who + how + where. If you are wrong, your team cannot accuse again for 15 minutes — and we announce it to everyone.0:45 开始，可以去 HQ 柜台指认凶手：讲出是谁 + 用什么方法杀的 + 在哪里杀的。猜错 = 15 分钟内不能再猜，还会广播给全场听。
  6. The first team to accuse correctly wins. At 2:15, every team hands in a final answer to decide the other places.谁第一个指认对，谁就赢。2:15 的时候，每一队都要交一个最终答案，排出剩下的名次。
  7. Don't break the props. Don't hide clue cards from your own teammates. Please be kind to the room masters.不要弄坏道具。不要跟自己队友藏线索卡。请对关主手下留情。

If players ask, answer like this (all crew)被问到就这样答（全体筹委/工作人员）

  • "Is X the killer?" → "That's for you to work out, detective."“X 是不是凶手？”→“这个要靠你自己查咯，侦探。”
  • Team stuck on your mini-game >10 min → give one small hint; >12 min → let them pass and give the clue, but tell them to hurry.小队卡在你的小游戏超过 10 分钟 → 给一个小提示点一点他们；超过 12 分钟 → 直接放行、给线索卡，但叫他们动作快点。
  • A team is missing or wandering around → call the floater on the radio.有小队不见了/在闲逛 → 用对讲机叫机动组。
  • Never confirm or deny any part of the answer — not even with your face.答案的任何部分都不能承认、也不能否认——用表情暗示也不行。
  • Never let a team take photos of another team's clue cards.绝对不能让一队去拍另一队的线索卡。
  • Never skip the mini-game "because they're nice kids."绝对不能因为“这些孩子蛮乖的”就跳过小游戏。

Ways to keep things fair and avoid chaos保持公平、避免乱场的几招

Hint desk (HQ)提示台（HQ）

Any team can buy a copy of any room's clue card. The price: a 10-minute wait (tell them "the darkroom is developing your film"). Write it on the scoreboard.任何小队都可以买任何房间线索卡的副本，代价是等 10 分钟（就说“暗房还在帮你冲洗照片”）。记得登记在计分板上。

15-minute ban猜错的惩罚

A wrong accusation = that team cannot accuse again for 15 minutes (the terminal blocks them). They can still play rooms.指认错了 = 那队 15 分钟内不能再猜（终端会自动挡住）。这段时间还是可以去闯关。

Pacing check进度检查

At the 1-hour mark, the floater counts how many rooms each team has cleared. Any team with fewer than 2 gets walked over to an empty room.满 1 小时的时候，机动组去数每队过了几间房。少过 2 间的队，就带他们去一间空房。

If a mini-game breaks小游戏出故障怎么办

Every station page has a backup way to play with no tech. Worst case: the room master asks the team 3 questions about the case so far, then gives them the clue.每个关卡页面都有不用器材的备用玩法。最坏的情况：关主就目前的案情问 3 道题，答完就把线索卡给他们。

Emergency contacts / decisions紧急情况找谁、谁说了算

  • Arguments about game rules → the HQ desk decides, the emcee announces it. No arguing again after that.游戏规则有争议 → HQ 台说了算，司仪宣布。宣布了就不再吵。
  • Someone is hurt / a real emergency → stop the game over the PA (say "ALL STATIONS, DEAD AIR, DEAD AIR"), all crew go to the main hall.有人受伤/真的出事 → 用广播叫停游戏（暗号保持英文：“ALL STATIONS, DEAD AIR, DEAD AIR”，意思是全部人停下、游戏中止），全体工作人员到大礼堂集合。
