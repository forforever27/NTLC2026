Rules briefed to players (emcee reads these)向营员宣读的规则（由司仪念）

  1. Stay with your team. Every room needs your whole team.全程跟紧你的小队。每个房间都需要全队到齐。
  2. One team per room. If occupied, go elsewhere — there is always a free room.一个房间同时只容一队。有人就换一间——总有空房。
  3. Win the room's challenge to earn its clue card. Each team can clear each room once.赢下房间的挑战才能拿到线索卡。每队每个房间只能通关一次。
  4. Suspects may be questioned but never forced. They will lie about only one thing: their guilt.嫌疑人可以盘问，但不可强迫。他们只会在一件事上说谎：自己有没有罪。
  5. Accusations at the HQ desk from 0:45: who + how + where. Wrong = 15-minute accusation ban, announced to everyone.0:45 起可到 HQ 指控台提出正式指控：嫌疑人 + 作案手法 + 案发地点。答错 = 15分钟禁赛（期间不得再指控），并向全场广播。
  6. First correct accusation wins. Everyone submits a final guess at 2:15 for the remaining placings.第一个指控正确的小队获胜。2:15 所有小队提交最终答案，决出其余名次。
  7. Don't destroy props. Don't hide clue cards from your own team. Have mercy on the room masters.不要破坏道具。不要对自己的队友藏线索卡。请对关主手下留情。

Standard operating answers (all crew)标准应答（全体筹委/工作人员）

  • "Is X the killer?" → "That's for you to work out, detective."“X 是不是凶手？”→“这就要靠你自己查了，侦探。”
  • Team stuck on your mini-game >10 min → give one nudge hint; >12 min → let them pass with the clue but tell them to move fast.小队卡在你的小游戏超过10分钟 → 给一个点拨式提示；超过12分钟 → 放行并给线索卡，但提醒他们抓紧时间。
  • Team missing / idle → radio the floater.有小队失踪/闲晃 → 用对讲机呼叫机动组。
  • Never confirm or deny any part of the solution, even by facial expression.绝不确认或否认答案的任何部分——连表情也不行。
  • Never let a team photograph another team's clue cards.绝不允许一队拍摄另一队的线索卡。
  • Never skip the mini-game "because they're nice kids."绝不因为“这些孩子挺乖的”就跳过小游戏。

Fairness & anti-chaos levers公平机制与防混乱开关

Hint desk (HQ)提示台（HQ）

Any team may buy a duplicate of any room's clue card for a 10-minute wait ("the darkroom is developing your film"). Log it on the scoreboard.任何小队都可以用等候10分钟换取任一房间线索卡的副本（“暗房正在帮你冲洗底片”）。记得登记在计分板上。

Lock-out禁赛

Wrong accusation = that team cannot accuse again for 15 minutes (the terminal enforces it). They can still play rooms.指控错误 = 该队15分钟内不得再次指控（由终端自动执行）。期间仍可继续闯关。

Pacing check进度检查

At the 1-hour mark the floater checks each team's cleared-room count. Any team below 2 clears gets shepherded to an empty room.满1小时时，机动组清点每队已通关的房间数。少于2间的队伍会被引导去一间空房。

If a mini-game breaks小游戏出故障怎么办

Every station page has a no-tech fallback. Worst case: room master quizzes the team with 3 questions about the case so far, then hands the clue.每个关卡页面都备有无科技的替代方案。最坏情况：关主就目前案情出3道问答题，答完就把线索卡交给他们。

Emergency contacts / decisions紧急联络与裁决

  • Game-rule disputes → HQ desk decides, emcee announces. No re-litigating.游戏规则争议 → 由 HQ 台裁决，司仪宣布。不得翻案。
  • Injury / real emergency → stop game via PA ("ALL STATIONS, DEAD AIR, DEAD AIR"), all crew to main hall.受伤/真正的紧急情况 → 通过广播叫停游戏（暗号保持英文：“ALL STATIONS, DEAD AIR, DEAD AIR”，意即全体停止、游戏中止），全体工作人员到大礼堂集合。
