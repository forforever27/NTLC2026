Everything for the murder mystery, in one place这个游戏的所有东西，都在这里

This hub exists so that everyone — not just the planner — knows the whole story and the whole game. Follow the three steps below. Reading time before camp: about 20 minutes total.这个中心的目的：让每一个筹委——不只是策划人——都知道完整的故事和玩法。照下面三步走。开营前要读的东西，总共大约 20 分钟。

Understand the game先看懂这个游戏

Read the Game Overview (how it plays, the map, the numbers), then the Case File (who really did it and how every clue works), then The Night Of (who was where, minute by minute — this is what keeps all our stories consistent).先读游戏总览（怎么玩、地图、关键数字），再读案件档案（真凶是谁、每条线索怎么运作），最后读案发之夜（每分钟谁在哪里——大家口径一致就靠它）。

Learn your own role找到你自己的角色

Find yourself in the directory below. Your page has everything: your character, your mini-game, your exact lines, what you must never say, and the props you prepare.在下面的名录里找到你自己。你的页面里什么都有：你的角色、你的小游戏、要讲的台词、绝对不能讲的东西、要准备的道具。

Prep, then run the day准备道具，然后跑当天流程

Shopping and printing: Props & Shopping → Station Props (every prop's full text) → Print Pack. On game day: the Run Sheet is the only page you need open, and the Flavor Pack makes it memorable.采购和打印：道具与采购 → 关卡道具文案（每件道具的完整文字）→ 打印包。活动当天：开着流程与规则这一页就够了，再配上气氛包让大家记住这一晚。
